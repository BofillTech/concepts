The BIBBQ Story

One of the world's all-time great comfort foods.

BIBBQ brings authentic Texas-style BBQ to Block Island. Set at the beautiful Sullivan House Inn, we strive to deliver a barbecue experience of the highest quality — every brisket trimmed by hand and smoked low and slow.

Whether you book a fun-filled BBQ under the marquee tent at the Sullivan House or order delivery across the island, you'll be certain to be satisfied.
