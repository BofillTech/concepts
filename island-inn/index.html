So Much More Than Just a Beach Trip

Atlantic Beach sits on North Carolina's stunning Crystal Coast — a haven for nature lovers, history buffs, and seafood enthusiasts. We're your basecamp for it all.

  • Fort Macon State Park — 2.3 miles
  • Pine Knoll Shores Aquarium — 10 min drive
  • Historic Beaufort — across the causeway
  • Shackleford Banks wild ponies
  • Deep sea fishing charters
  • Crystal Coast Brewing Company — next door
