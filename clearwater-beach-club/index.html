Welcome to Clearwater

New York's Best-Kept
Summer Secret

Clearwater Beach Club is a private membership club set along the majestic Atlantic Ocean in Atlantic Beach, New York — offering Long Island's largest private beach, where families return summer after summer to create memories that last a lifetime.

From ocean-front cabanas to an Olympic-size pool, every detail is crafted for total service in a family-casual atmosphere. Like a summer vacation right in your own backyard.

Ocean Front Cabanas

Steps from the Atlantic, with private changing rooms and hot & cold running water. No foot traffic. Attendants at your service all day.

Court Cabanas

Pool Lockers

The Beach

Long Island's Largest
Private Beach

Atlantic Beach is a small, beautiful village on the Atlantic Ocean — accessible only to residents and beach club members. Just 24 miles from the Queens Midtown Tunnel, your perfect beach day is closer than you think.
