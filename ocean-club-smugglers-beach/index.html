Welcome to the Cape

A home on the water, the old Cape Cod way.

Set right on Smuggler's Beach at the mouth of the Bass River, The Ocean Club is the kind of place you settle into. Days are made for swimming, sunning, long walks on the sand and the simple business of relaxing.

Our one- and two-bedroom suites pair the comfort of home with the easy excitement of a Cape Cod vacation — each one fully equipped, each one with a water view.

Find Us

On Smuggler's Beach, at the mouth of the Bass River.

Smuggler's Beach is a noteworthy Cape Cod landmark — a wide sandy beach with calm waves and ample parking, just a few footsteps from your door. Venture out for mini-golf, the Judah Baker Windmill, the shops and seafood of South Yarmouth, or the harbors of Hyannis — there's a little something for everyone.
