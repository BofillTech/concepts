Welcome

A bright, easy base for all of San Diego.

Just off Interstate 5 at the Palomar Street exit, the Palomar Inn puts the whole of San Diego County within easy reach — the Convention Center, the Cruise Terminal and the International Airport are all only minutes away.

Our rooms and deluxe mini-suites have been newly renovated to a higher standard, and the trolley north into the city or south toward the Mexico border is just one block from our door. Ocean-view rooms are available on request.
