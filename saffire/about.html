Our Story

Where the Beach Meets Bangkok

A Thai beach bar at the edge of the Atlantic.

— Est. 2026 · Narragansett, RI —

Born from a love of Thai flavors and the Rhode Island coast.

Saffire began with a simple idea: the open, herbal, fire-driven cooking of Thailand belongs at the edge of the ocean. Narragansett summers have always been about long evenings, salt air, and food meant to be shared — and Thai cuisine speaks that language fluently.

We built Saffire to bring those two worlds together. Wok fire and grilled lemongrass meet Point Judith fluke, local farm produce, and the freshest catch the docks bring in. Our cocktail program follows the same compass — Thai basil, kaffir lime, and bird chili shaken with care, made to drink slow as the sun goes down.

One Beach Street is just steps from the water. Whether you stop in barefoot after a day at the beach or settle in for a long dinner with friends, you'll find a kitchen that's ambitious, a bar that's playful, and a room that's built to welcome you back again.

Our Philosophy

✦

Fresh Ingredients

Local sourcing, seasonal menus, Point Judith partnerships. Our kitchen leans on what's coming off the boats and out of Rhode Island farms.

◇

Bold Flavors

Authentic Thai techniques, house-made curry pastes, and the four-pillar balance — sweet, sour, salty, spicy — built into every dish.

∽

Beach-Town Hospitality

Relaxed, welcoming, barefoot-friendly. We're a beach bar first — come as you are, stay as long as you want.

◈

Craft Cocktails

Thai-inspired mixology with house-made syrups, fresh herbs, and zero-proof options that hold their own next to anything on the back bar.
