— Welcome to Saffire —

Where the Beach Meets Bangkok

Tucked along Narragansett's beach district at One Beach Street, Saffire pairs the bold, layered flavors of Thai cuisine with the easy rhythm of a New England summer. Local seafood from Point Judith, herbs and produce from Rhode Island farms, and craft cocktails built around lemongrass, kaffir lime, and Thai chili — all served against the sound of the Atlantic.
