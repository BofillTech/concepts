What's Happening at Saffire

From live music on the patio to once-a-year holiday menus — here's what's on.

Every Thu

Live Music Nights

7:00 PM – 10:00 PM · In-Season

Local musicians take over the patio every Thursday all summer long. No cover, no reservation needed for the bar — book a table for dinner with a soundtrack.

Monthly Pop

Thai Street Food Pop-Ups

First Sunday of the Month

One night, one menu, one street vendor's lifelong recipes. Past pop-ups have featured boat noodles, khao soi, and southern-style fried chicken. Tickets release two weeks ahead.

Quarterly Class

Cocktail Masterclasses

Saturdays · 2:00 PM

Two hours behind the bar with our beverage team. Learn three signature builds, take home a recipe card, and stay for dinner with a curated welcome flight.

Apr 13

Songkran — Thai New Year

All Day · Special Menu

Our biggest celebration of the year. Special prix-fixe menu, traditional sweets, and a few surprises. Reservations open 30 days in advance and book up fast.

Dec 31

New Year's Eve

Two Seatings · 5:30 PM & 9:00 PM

Five courses, a midnight cocktail, and the kind of room where strangers end up at the same table by the end of the night. Limited seating.
