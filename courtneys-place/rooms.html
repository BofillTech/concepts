A few notes —

Seasons, deposits, & the fine print.

Off Season

Jul 1 – Oct 15. The quietest stretch — when locals call it our island again.

Shoulder Season

Apr 16 – Jun 30 & Oct 16 – Dec 20. Warm days, lighter crowds — our favourite.

High Season

Dec 21 – Apr 15. Sunshine, fantasy fest, and reasons to book early.

Cancellation

21 days prior for regular dates; 60 days for holidays and special events. Travel insurance recommended.

Rates may be higher during Fantasy Fest and other special events. All rates double occupancy unless noted. Pet fees apply.
