I

an invitation

A quiet lane in the heart of Old Key West.

We're tucked off Simonton — a short, signposted walk from Duval, but a different gear entirely. Roosters in the morning. Bougainvillea everywhere. Cottages older than most things on this island.

Linda & Chuck Krumel built Courtney's Place as a legacy to their daughter, Courtney, who now owns and operates the property. The staff are family. The pool's small on purpose. Breakfast is set out each morning and you take it back to your porch.

We're people-friendly, kid-friendly, pet-friendly — in fact we're just plain friendly. Come unwind with us. Feel like a guest, not a tourist.

III

what's included

Small island. Big hospitality.

No room service, no concierge desk, no glass-walled lobby. What you get instead: a key, a porch, a pool, a bicycle, breakfast, and the run of Old Key West.

i.

The Plunge Pool

Small on purpose — the kind you actually use, in a gated tropical courtyard surrounded by frangipani and palm shade.

ii.

Bikes On Premise

Rent a beach cruiser at the office. Pedal to Mallory Square, the Hemingway House, or just to dinner.

iii.

Continental Breakfast

Set out each morning. Take it back to your porch and wait for the rooster to introduce himself.

iv.

Free Parking

Off-street, on premise, gated. A small luxury in this part of the island.

v.

Tropical Courtyard

Bougainvillea, frangipani, palm shade. A BBQ area when you'd rather grill in than dine out.

vi.

Pet Friendly

Bring the dog. We've put a few of our guests' four-legged friends on the website.

vii.

Cottages Equipped

Full kitchens in cottages, kitchenettes in efficiencies. Stay in for the night, or stay in for the week.

viii.

A/C, Wi-Fi, Cable, Daily Housekeeping

The basics, handled. Plus free local calls, irons and hair dryers at the office.
