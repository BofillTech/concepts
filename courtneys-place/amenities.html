Home/Amenities

small island, big hospitality —

What's included,
what's around.

No room service, no concierge desk, no glass-walled lobby. What you get instead: a key, a porch, a pool, a bicycle, breakfast, and the run of Old Key West.

On the property

The lane, and the lane's perks.

Everything inside the gate is yours. Pool, courtyard, BBQ, bikes — and parking off the street, which on this end of Old Town is a small luxury.

The Plunge Pool

Small on purpose — the kind you actually use. Set in a gated tropical courtyard surrounded by frangipani and palm shade. Heated in the cooler months.

Bikes on Premise

Rent a beach cruiser at the front office. Pedal to Mallory Square, Smathers Beach, or just to dinner. Locks, baskets, and the occasional bell included.

Extended Breakfast

Continental breakfast is set out each morning — coffee, pastries, fresh fruit, juices. Take it back to your porch, eat at a courtyard table, or carry it to the pool.

Tropical Courtyard

Bougainvillea, frangipani, palm shade. A BBQ area when you'd rather grill in than dine out — and benches that catch the sea breeze just so.

Free Parking

Off-street, gated, on premise. A genuine luxury at this end of Old Town. Note: spots are first-come; a few are tight — the trade-off for being inside the gate.

Pet Friendly

Bring the dog. Honestly. We've put a few of our guests' four-legged friends on the website. A reasonable pet fee applies — see the policy section below for the details.

In your cottage

Fully equipped. Genuinely livable.

Cottages have full kitchens — refrigerator, range, oven, microwave, coffee maker, dishes, pots, the works. Efficiencies have kitchenettes. Private rooms get coffee makers, microwaves, and mini fridges.

Every quarter at Courtney's Place has its own private entrance — no tip-toeing through someone else's hallway. Daily housekeeping. Free local calls. Wi-Fi throughout the property. Cable TV. The basics, handled — so you can stop thinking about them.

At the office

A real front desk. With a real local behind it.

Our reservation desk is open 9 am – 5 pm daily (2 pm during holidays and special events). The staff is family — most of us have lived on the Island for years, some of us were born here.

We can point you toward the best Cuban sandwich on Whitehead, the quietest snorkel spot, the bicycle shop that doesn't gouge tourists, and the bar where Tennessee Williams used to drink — depending on what kind of evening you're after.

And if your flight runs late or your boat needs to leave at dawn, just tell us. We'll figure it out.

The fine print

Policies and the rest.

Plain English. No surprises at check-out. If anything's unclear, the office picks up — just call us.

Check-in / Check-out

Check-in 3 pm. Check-out 11 am. Early arrival? Drop your bags, take a bike, come back when your cottage is ready.

Cancellation

21 days prior for regular dates; 60 days prior for holidays and special events. Deposit refunded less $30. No refunds for early departures. Travel insurance recommended.

Pets

Pet friendly with a reasonable per-stay fee. Most rooms welcome dogs. Please let us know in advance — we'll tell you which cottages are best for which size.

Kids

Family friendly. Two-bedroom cottages are best for groups; pool is kid-safe with adult supervision. Cribs and pack-n-plays available on request.

Smoking

All cottages, studios, and rooms are non-smoking. Smoking is permitted only in the designated courtyard area, away from windows and entrances.

Quiet Hours

11 pm – 8 am. The neighbourhood is residential and a lot of our guests are repeat visitors. Be the kind of neighbour you'd want next door.

Holidays / Special Events

Rates may be higher during Fantasy Fest and major holidays. Minimum stays apply during peak weekends — call the office for details.

Deposits

Reservations require a deposit at booking. Reservations made within 21 days of arrival require non-refundable payment in full.
