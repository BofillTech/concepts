A Quiet Bay-Side Retreat

Three decades of quietly perfect summers.

Built in the 1920s and under the same family ownership for over thirty years, the Mattituck Motel is the kind of place generations come back to. Just one block from the bay. Five minutes on foot to the sand. And a short drive to nearly every winery on the Fork.

No pretense. No noise. Just clean, comfortable rooms, a quiet lawn, a fire pit at dusk, and a pair of beach chairs ready when you are.

The Beach

Five minutes on foot to Peconic Bay.

The beach is just up our quiet street — perfect for swimming, fishing, and the kind of slow afternoon you forgot was possible. We keep beach chairs on hand for guests who didn't pack their own.

Wine Country

A short drive from every tasting room you've meant to visit.
