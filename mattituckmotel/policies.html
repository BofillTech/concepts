No. 01

Reservations & Deposits

We take reservations by phone at 631-298-4131. We don't list rates online — every stay's a little different, and we'd rather quote you fairly on the call than post a price that doesn't fit your dates.

A valid credit card holds your reservation. We charge one night's rate as a deposit at booking; the balance is settled at check-in. If you'd rather pay the full amount up front, just say so.

No. 02

Cancellation

Plans change — we get it. Here's the rough shape of our policy:

  • 7 days or more before arrival: full refund of your deposit.
  • Inside 7 days: we keep the one-night deposit.
  • Peak summer weekends & holiday windows: the cancellation window may be longer (typically 14 days). We'll tell you on the call.
  • No-show: the full reservation is charged.

If something serious comes up — weather, illness, family emergency — call us. We've been doing this a long time and we try to be reasonable.

No. 03

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in: 3:00pm. The front desk runs daily from 9:00am to 11:30pm during our season.

Check-out: 11:00am. Late check-out is usually possible — ask at the desk when you arrive and we'll let you know.

Arriving early? You're welcome to drop your bags at the front desk and head straight to the beach. Arriving late? Call us and we'll figure out the after-hours hand-off.

No. 04

Payment

We accept all major credit and debit cards. The card you used to book is the card we'll charge the balance to at check-in unless you tell us otherwise.

Incidentals — if any — are billed at check-out. We don't charge resort fees, parking fees, or WiFi fees. The rate we quote is the rate you pay.

No. 05

Stays & Occupancy

Our rooms come in two flavors:

  • Two Bed Double Queen: two queen beds, sleeps up to 4.
  • King: one king bed, sleeps 2.

Minimum-stay rules may apply on summer Saturdays and holiday weekends — typically 2 to 3 nights. Children are welcome in any room within the occupancy limit. If you need a portable crib or extra bedding, mention it when you book.

No. 06

Smoking

All rooms are non-smoking, including covered porches and indoor common areas. Smoking is welcome in the open outdoor areas of the grounds — we just ask that you use the receptacles provided.

Smoking inside a room triggers a deep-cleaning fee that goes to the housekeeping team. We'd rather not charge it, so please step outside.

No. 07

Pets

We love dogs. We don't yet have a formal pet program, so if you're hoping to travel with one, please call before you book. We'll let you know what's possible, what room would work, and what we'd ask of you (and them).

Service animals are always welcome, no questions asked, in line with ADA guidelines.

No. 08

Quiet Hours

Quiet hours are 10:00pm to 8:00am. The whole reason people come back to us year after year is that we've kept the place quiet on purpose — please help us keep it that way.

The fire pit and outdoor seating are open until 10pm. After that, we'd appreciate keeping conversations and music low so your neighbors can sleep.

No. 09

Accessibility

We're an older property — built in the 1920s, lovingly maintained, but not purpose-designed for full ADA compliance. That said, we have ground-floor rooms with step-in access and we're happy to talk through specific needs before you book.

If mobility, hearing, or visual accommodations matter for your stay, call us in advance. We'll be straightforward about what we can and can't offer.

No. 10

Seasonal Operations

The motel is open April through December. Off-season (January through March), the front desk is closed and we don't take new bookings — but you can still leave a message or send an inquiry through the contact form, and we'll get back to you when we reopen.

Severe weather is a part of life out here. If a named storm threatens to disrupt your stay, we'll be in touch about rescheduling or refunding — we don't penalize guests for honest acts of nature.

Policies are confirmed at the time of booking and may vary by season or room type. When in doubt, call us — we'll talk you through it.

Ready When You Are

Anything else, just ask.

Phone is fastest — we'll talk through any policy question and quote your dates while we're at it.
