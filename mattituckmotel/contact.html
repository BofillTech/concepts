Getting Here

A quick run off Bay Avenue.

From the LIE: Take Route 25 East all the way through Mattituck. Just past the village, turn south onto Bay Avenue. We're a few minutes down on the left, just before the water. Coming from Greenport, head west on Route 25 and turn left onto Bay Avenue.

Free parking right outside your room. If you'd rather take the train, the LIRR drops you in Mattituck Village — about a mile away.
