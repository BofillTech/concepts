The Rooms

Comfortably old school.

Our rooms have a quiet mid-century cabin feel — clean, simple, and refreshingly free of fuss. Each opens onto the same scenic, park-like grounds, with the bay just up our quiet street.

Daily housekeeping. Free WiFi and parking. A/C in every room. Beach chairs whenever you need them. Tell us when you're arriving and what you've got planned — we'll do the rest.
