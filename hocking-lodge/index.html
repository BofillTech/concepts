A Perfect Getaway

Rustic warmth, set in the heart of the Hocking Hills.

Tucked into the scenic hills of southeastern Ohio, The Lodge blends modern comfort with a rustic spirit drawn straight from the forest around it.

A fireside lobby welcomes you year-round. Each room is furnished with an aspen-timber bed and live-edge oak tables — joined by branch fixtures, barn doors and the Lodge's signature emerald leaflet sinks — for a setting made for slowing down.
