Our Story

Owned by three
lifelong locals.
Run by people you'll remember.

The Miles City Hotel & Suites was previously part of a national hotel chain franchise. When three lifelong residents of Miles City took ownership, they did it with a single goal: make this the very best hotel not just in eastern Montana, but in all of Montana.

That commitment shows up in the rooms, the breakfast, the lobby, and most importantly, in the people on staff — all of whom are local folks, participating in extensive ongoing training. From custodial work to food preparation to guest accommodation, the standard is the same: do it like you'd want it done in your own home.
