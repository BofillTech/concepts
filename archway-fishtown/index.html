Guest Services

Considered, never intrusive

City Fitness

A block away, with cutting-edge equipment and programming. Every Archway guest enjoys complimentary gym pass access.

Dining at the Door

Sister restaurant Suraya sits directly across the street; the award-winning Kalaya is blocks north. We're glad to help arrange reservations with notice.

Time to Spare

Check-in 4PM, check-out 11AM. When availability allows, extend with early arrival from 2PM or late departure to 1PM for $50.

Flowers

Arrange a room delivery through our favorite local purveyors, Walter Pine. Just ask our guest services team.

Luggage Storage

Secured, complimentary storage on the ground floor for up to ten hours — arrive early, leave late, see the city unburdened.

Parking

A limited number of overnight spots are available to guests for a nightly fee. Reach out to reserve yours.
