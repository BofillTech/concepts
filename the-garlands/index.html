Open your doors — the ocean is right there.

Tucked along the quiet shore of Nantucket Sound, The Garlands is a small, family-owned collection of twenty one- and two-bedroom condos — each one different, each one ours. Private balconies and patios open directly to the water views. Kitchenettes mean mornings at your own pace. And just outside your door, a wide oceanfront sundeck leads down to your own stretch of beach.

There's no rush here. No minimum stay. Come for a week, a weekend, or one quiet night when you just need to hear the tide. We'll be ready.

Just for you

Our beach is private.
And it stays that way.

From the sundeck, a short flight of stairs takes you straight onto a quiet stretch of Nantucket Sound — reserved for guests of The Garlands.

No crowds. No fight for a spot. Just warm Sound water, soft sand, and a chair waiting for you.

A Day on the Sound

The long, slow rhythm
of a week at the water.

Morning · 6:47 a.m.

First light, straight through
the balcony door.

The sun finds your private balcony before it finds anything else. You bring your coffee, a book you'll barely open, and the whole morning ahead of you. Low tide today — you can see all the way to the shoals.

Afternoon · 2:15 p.m.

Down the stairs.
Onto our private beach.

The oceanfront sundeck leads right down to the sand. The kids have been in and out of the water since lunch. Someone is trying to teach them to sail, or at least hold a line. The Sound is calm today.

Evening · 7:22 p.m.

The sky does what
it does here.

A glass of something cold, a chair turned toward the water, and that long Cape Cod dusk that makes the whole summer feel endless. Dinner is whenever — the kitchen's right there.
