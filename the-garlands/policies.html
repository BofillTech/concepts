Policies & Stay Info

Everything you need to know before you pack the car.

We keep things simple and friendly here. A few quick details so your stay starts smoothly — and please don't hesitate to call if you have any questions.

Arrival & Departure

Check-in & Check-out

Check In
3:00 pm
Check Out
10:00 am

A valid photo ID and the credit card used for booking are required at check-in.

Early arrivals and late check-outs may be available on request — please call ahead and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Length of Stay

No minimum stay

Stay one night or one month — we welcome you anytime. There's no minimum stay required at any time of year.

Peak summer weeks (July & August) tend to book up early, so reserve in advance for your dream dates.

Cancellations

Plans change. We get it.

Cancellation terms vary by booking source and unit type. Please review the policy on your reservation confirmation.

For direct bookings, contact us as soon as possible if you need to change your dates. We'll work with you wherever we can.

Children

Family welcome

Children of all ages are welcome at The Garlands. Our condos are well-suited to family stays, with full kitchens and space to spread out.

Cribs and extra beds aren't available on-site, so please plan accordingly.

Pets

No pets, please

We love animals, but pets aren't permitted at The Garlands. Service animals are accommodated in line with applicable law.

Smoking

Smoke-free indoors

All units are non-smoking. We have a designated outdoor smoking area for guests who prefer to step out.

Cleaning fees apply if smoking occurs inside a unit or on a balcony.

Parking

Free, on-site

Free parking is included for all guests. One spot per unit; please check with the office if you need additional space for visitors.

Beach Access

For guests only

Our private beach and oceanfront sundeck are reserved for registered guests of The Garlands. Day visitors and outside guests are not permitted on the beach.

Please be considerate of your neighbors and pack out what you bring in.

Quiet Hours

Peace & quiet

Quiet hours are 10:00 pm to 8:00 am. Many of our guests come for the calm — we'd appreciate your help keeping it that way.

Please keep music and conversations on balconies and the sundeck mindful of nearby units after dark.

Damage & Care

Treat it like home

Each unit is individually owned, and we take pride in keeping them in great condition. Guests are responsible for any damage beyond normal wear during their stay.

Please report any issues to the office right away so we can help.

Wi-Fi & Connectivity

Stay connected

Complimentary Wi-Fi is available in all units, along with cable TV. Connectivity is reliable for streaming, video calls, and remote work.

Network details will be provided at check-in.

Accessibility

Reach out

Our property includes outdoor stairs and decks; some units are easier to access than others. Please call ahead to discuss accessibility needs — we'll match you with the unit that works best.

Still have questions?

We're happy to help.

Anything not covered here? Give us a call or send a quick email — we'll get back to you the same day during business hours.
