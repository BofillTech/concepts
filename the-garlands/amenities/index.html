Amenities & Features

The simple things, done well. Everything you actually need for a quiet week by the water.

Where comfort meets the coast

A small place that thinks of everything.

Twenty oceanfront and ocean-view condos, a private beach, a generous oceanfront sundeck, and a Cape Cod town with whale-watching, day trips, and good food just minutes away. Here's everything that comes with your stay.

In Your Unit

A real kitchen, your own balcony, and the air just right.

Every condo at The Garlands is set up for actual living — not just sleeping. Cook breakfast at your own pace, work or stream from anywhere in the unit, and crack the slider to a balcony or patio when you need a minute with the water.

  • Air conditioning in every unit Newly added across the entire property — every unit stays cool through the hottest Cape Cod afternoons.
  • Full kitchenette Stovetop, microwave, fridge, coffee maker, dishes, and pans. Everything you need for groceries-not-takeout mornings and family dinners by the water.
  • Cable TV & Wi-Fi Reliable connectivity throughout the property — for work-from-water days, rainy-afternoon movies, and keeping the kids occupied between beach runs.
  • Private patio or balcony Most face the Sound directly, others look over the lawn or sundeck. Either way, the door opens to the salt air.
  • Private bathroom Full bath in every unit. No shared spaces, no hallway shuffles — the whole place is yours.
On the Property

A private beach, an oceanfront sundeck, and the whole Sound in front of you.

The Garlands sits on a quiet stretch of Nantucket Sound with the kind of warm, calm swimming water Cape Cod's south shore is famous for. Step off the deck and you're on the sand — and the sand is yours.

  • Private beach Reserved for registered guests. No crowds, no parking battles, no fight for a spot — just a clean stretch of Nantucket Sound, soft sand, and a chair waiting for you.
  • Oceanfront sundeck A generous shared deck with chairs and chaise lounges that look straight out at the Sound. Coffee in the morning, cocktails at sunset, kids in and out from the beach below.
  • Direct beach access Stairs from the sundeck go straight to the sand. No streets to cross, no walks to the water — the kids can come and go all day.
  • Generous lawn & outdoor seating Adirondack chairs, picnic tables, and quiet spots to read. Plenty of room for a beach day that doesn't actually involve sand.
  • Free, on-site parking One spot per unit, included. Pull in, unload, and don't move the car again until you have to.
