Welcome inside

Each unit is different.
All of them are home.

The Garlands is a small, family-owned property of twenty one- and two-bedroom condos — each one individually owned and appointed. Some sit right on the water, some are a few steps in. All come with the space and quiet of home, full kitchens for cooking at your own pace, and that long Cape Cod summer feel.

Front Row

Private balconies that open directly to Nantucket Sound views. The sound of the tide is your soundtrack from wake to dusk — a full sweep of water as far as you can see.
