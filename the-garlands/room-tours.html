How to read this page

Three things to know before you book.

Every Garlands condo has the same essentials — full kitchen, private bath, A/C, Wi-Fi, balcony or patio. The differences come down to bedroom count and which way you face. The badges on each card make it quick to scan.

  • Ocean Front
    Direct waterfront Balconies face Nantucket Sound directly — the closest thing to floating.
  • Ocean View
    Sound views, set back A few steps from the front row. Same property, same beach, slightly less of a price tag.
  • 2BR 1BR
    One or two bedrooms Most units are two-bedroom. Unit D is our single one-bedroom — great for couples or quick getaways.
