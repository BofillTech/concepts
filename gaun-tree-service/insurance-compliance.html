Now offering

Carriers are tightening up.
We’ll help you stay covered.

In the last year, homeowner’s insurance companies across Northern Arizona have started requiring documented defensible-space mitigation as a condition of policy renewal. Non-compliant properties are getting non-renewal letters. We’ve been doing this work since 1992 — now we package it for the carrier.

Why now

The wildland-urban interface changed the calculus.

Wildfire losses across the West have driven insurance carriers to push risk back to the homeowner. In Northern Arizona, that’s showing up at the mailbox.

  1. 01

    Non-renewal letters are real

    Carriers are issuing non-renewal notices on properties they consider too high-risk. Compliance is no longer an option — it’s the only way to stay covered.

  2. 02

    Defensible-space requirements have teeth

    0–5 ft, 5–30 ft, and 30–100 ft zones are no longer suggestions. Carriers and CAL FIRE-aligned standards expect documented work in each.

  3. 03

    Documentation matters as much as the work

    It’s not enough to do the mitigation — you have to be able to prove it to the underwriter. Photos, scope letters, and clean paperwork.

  4. 04

    Fly-by-night operators won’t pass

    Carriers will not accept work from uninsured operators. The license, the workers’ comp, and the general liability behind our name are part of why our work counts.

How it works

Assess. Document. Execute. Renew.

A four-step process designed to satisfy a carrier and protect a home.

I.

Property Assessment

We walk your full defensible-space envelope. Identify ladder fuels, canopy continuity, dead and diseased material, and proximity hazards. We benchmark against current carrier and Firewise standards.

II.

Carrier-Spec Documentation

Written scope of work, before-photos, hazard map. The package an underwriter expects to see — formatted so they can approve without coming back with questions.

III.

Defensible-Space Remediation

Strategic thinning, ladder-fuel removal, dead and dying material out, slash hauled off. We do the work to the spec — not over, not under.

IV.

Renewal Support

After-photos, completion letter, and follow-up package. If your carrier or agent has questions, we’ll be on the call.

Who should call us
