Where we work

Six communities. One county.

  1. 01 HQ

    Flagstaff

    35.1983° N, 111.6513° W · Elev. 7,000 ft

    Home turf since 1992. Full crew, same-day response on most jobs, and the yard is fifteen minutes from most of town. The bulk of our work — residential, municipal, commercial, and emergency.

    • Same-day service common
    • All service lines available
    • Full equipment access
  2. 02

    Williams

    35.2495° N, 112.1908° W · 35 mi west

    Forty minutes west on I-40. Heavy ponderosa cover and a lot of WUI properties. Common work here: Firewise thinning, hazardous removals, and lot clearing on outlying parcels.

    • 40 min from yard
    • Firewise & insurance compliance heavy
    • Scheduled response within 48 hours typical
  3. 03

    Sedona & Oak Creek

    34.8697° N, 111.7610° W · 28 mi south

    Down the canyon on 89A. Mixed forest, high-value residential, and a lot of complex removals where access is tight. Crane and man-lift work is common here.

    • 45 min from yard via 89A
    • Crane & technical removals common
    • Estate & resort work
  4. 04

    Munds Park

    34.9344° N, 111.6353° W · 21 mi south

    Twenty minutes south on I-17. Dense pine, second-home properties, and a lot of seasonal owners who need work done while they’re out of town. We coordinate access and report back.

    • 25 min from yard
    • Seasonal-owner friendly
    • Firewise & pruning common
  5. 05

    Bellemont

    35.2333° N, 111.8333° W · 13 mi west

    Just west of town on I-40. Mixed residential and light commercial. Quick out-and-back from the yard — easy on the schedule.

    • 20 min from yard
    • Same-day for emergencies
    • Lot clearing & light commercial
  6. 06

    Greater Coconino County

    All of Northern Arizona within reach

    Doney Park, Mountainaire, Kachina Village, Parks, Tusayan, Valle, and out to the rim. If your property is in Coconino County, call us — most of it we can get to. Some larger jobs require travel time, but the work is the same.

    • Travel-time quoted on estimate
    • Larger jobs preferred for distant locations
    • Emergency response considered case-by-case
