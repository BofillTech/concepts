What’s Included

No surprises.
No silly fees.

Free Wi-Fi, free cable, free parking for the car — and the boat. A real on-site café. A front desk staffed by real humans.

  1. Home
  2. Amenities

01

Boat & Trailer Parking — free, on-site

If you came to the Keys to fish or to dive, this is the one that matters. We've got the space for your boat and your trailer alongside the car — no separate lot, no surcharge, no asterisk. Roll in with all of it, park it, sleep it off, and roll out the next morning.

No fee

02

On-Site Café — breakfast & lunch

There's a café right on the property serving breakfast and lunch — coffee in the morning, sandwiches and lunch fare the rest of the day. Probably the shortest commute to a hot meal in the Lower Keys. You don't have to find a car to find food.

On site

03

Free Wi-Fi — throughout

Every room, the parking lot, the lawn out front. Strong enough to actually do work or stream the game. You shouldn't have to pay for the internet at a motel in 2026.

No fee

04

Free Cable TV — full lineup

Full cable in every room. ESPN, the local channels, the movie channels, the news. We don't strip it down to a "basic" tier and call it free. It's the real thing.

No fee

05

Free Parking — the car too

It's worth saying out loud: parking is free for the car, even if you're not bringing a boat. No nightly resort fee, no "valet" pretending to be optional. Just pull in.

No fee

06

Front Desk — 8 AM to 10 PM

Real humans, every day. Open seven days from eight in the morning until ten at night. No QR-code-only check-in, no kiosk, no app to download. If you have a question, someone answers.

Daily

07

Non-Smoking — every room

The whole property is non-smoking. Cleaner air in the room, cleaner linens, cleaner stay all around. Smoke outside if you need to, please.

All rooms

08

Pet-Free — no exceptions

We don't allow pets in the rooms. We know it's a hard policy — it's also why our rooms are clean and free of allergens. The Key Deer outside will be thrilled to take their place.

No pets
