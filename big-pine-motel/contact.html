    Getting Here

    From the nearest airports.

    Two airports, two very different drives. Both are scenic. One is shorter.

    ~129 mi · ~3 hours

    From Miami International (MIA)

    South on the Florida Turnpike to the Homestead extension, then onto US-1 South. From there it's one road all the way down — through Key Largo, Islamorada, Marathon, across the 7 Mile Bridge. Big Pine Key is the second island past the bridge. We're on the right at Mile Marker 30.

    Tip: The drive is the point. Plan to stop for stone crab in Islamorada or a sunset at Anne's Beach.

    ~32 mi · ~45 min

    From Key West International (EYW)

    North on US-1 from Key West. Drive across Boca Chica, Big Coppitt, Sugarloaf, Cudjoe, Summerland, Ramrod, Little Torch — and then Big Pine. We're on the left side of US-1 at Mile Marker 30 if you're driving from Key West.

    Tip: Easier drive than from Miami — cheaper flights into Miami, though. Run the numbers.
