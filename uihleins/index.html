A Working Waterfront

The same family,
the same dock,
for over seventy years.

Henry Uihlein opened the marina with twenty-four sixteen-foot wooden skiffs and a small motel on the harbor. His son grew up sweeping the dock, learning to paint hulls, and waiting on customers between college summers.

Today, Henry Jr. runs the place — and his son Henry works the dock between college breaks, just as his father did. A family business in the truest sense, built on long days, fair prices, and a deep love of the water.

— The Uihlein Family Three generations · Montauk, NY

Seven Efficiencies
Fully Renovated

A small motel with a putting green on the harbor.

Seven efficiency rooms — each with a queen bed, a twin, a full kitchenette, dining area, bath and shower. Floor-to-ceiling renovated. Flat-screen TV, A/C, Wi-Fi. Walk to the docks, the bait shop, and dinner.

The grounds are quietly Montauk: BBQs, a putting green, a small mini-golf course, a play area for the kids, and plenty of room to relax after a day on the water.

The Montauk Mercury
Grand Slam Fishing Tournament.

Hosted at Uihlein's every summer — a Montauk tradition that benefits the Montauk Friends of Erin and the East Hampton Kiwanis Club. Henry Jr. has been a member of both for years; one of the few tournaments where the prizes go back into the community.

Captains, anglers, and the whole family welcome. Watch the weigh-in at the dock at the end of the day — that part is free.
