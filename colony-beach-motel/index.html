Welcome

The charm of old Cape Cod, just steps from the sand.

Nestled by the beach overlooking picturesque Nantucket Sound, the Colony Beach Motel welcomes you to a fabulous vacation setting in the heart of Cape Cod.

Combining the charm of old world Colonial architecture with the convenience of modern resort facilities, our newly renovated property offers everything good under the Cape Cod sun. Bask on our private beach, refresh in the warm waters of Nantucket Sound, or take a swim in our heated pool.

Property

Everything good under the Cape Cod sun.

  • Private Beach

    Direct access to a quiet stretch of Nantucket Sound sand, just outside your door.

  • Heated Pool

    An outdoor heated pool with a spacious patio and sundeck overlooking the water.

  • BBQ Grills

    Gas grills on the patio for casual evenings — picture an oceanside cookout under the sun.

  • Changing & Laundry

    On-site changing room and laundry facilities for longer stays and post-beach refreshes.

  • Sundeck & Patio

    A spacious poolside patio and sundeck with the Sound stretching out behind it.

  • In-Room Comforts

    Individual heat & A/C, cable TV, refrigerator, tile bath, and picture windows in every room.
