Our Story

It Started on a Farm

Bare Naked Bakery was founded on a small farm in East Meadow, New York in 2008. We believe that food should be safe and delicious in an environment that's warm and cozy.

Creating a safe place for our guests — especially kids — to enjoy food without worry is the primary aim here. You'll hear the chopping of fruits and vegetables, smell our homemade cookies in the oven, and see our bakers working around the clock.

Over 15 years later, we continue to fulfill the needs of our guests with a menu of farm fresh, prepared-to-order food on our home-baked breads and dough. These are the traditions we established on a farm years ago that we still passionately follow today.
