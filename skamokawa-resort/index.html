A getaway unlike any other on the river

Tucked into the Skamokawa National Historic District.

Our resort sits just outside Cathlamet, Washington, about ninety minutes northwest of Portland on beautiful State Route 4. In a picturesque riverside setting you’ll find eight hotel rooms, three luxury vacation rentals, a conference center, a large dock and a fully stocked general store — every one of them directly on the water.

It’s the perfect place for a romantic getaway, a family reunion, a wedding reception or a quiet weekend away — with adventure right outside the door and stillness whenever you want it.

— Lance & Shannon Britt, owners

Right outside the door

Adventure, or none at all.

If you’re looking for adventure you won’t have to look far. And if you’re looking for stillness, you’ll find that too.

  • Guided Kayaking

    World-class guided tours for every skill level, a short walk away with Columbia River Kayaking.

  • Paddleboarding

    Push off from the dock and glide the calm, sheltered waters of the slough at your own pace.

  • World-Class Fishing

    Some of the finest fishing on the lower Columbia is right at your doorstep, plus hunting nearby.

  • Bird Watching

    A quiet riverside perch for watching herons, eagles and the ships that pass on the channel.

  • The Large Dock

    A generous dock for launching kayaks and boards, tying up, or simply watching the water move.

  • The General Store

    Fully stocked with the essentials — and the bottle of wine for an evening by the fire.

Moments away: Vista Park beach access, Skamokawa Farmstead Creamery, two disc-golf courses, Skyline Golf Course, and the Friends of Skamokawa Interpretive Center across the street.
