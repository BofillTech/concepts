Welcome to the Peninsula

A warm Texas hello, right on the Gulf.

Our deluxe studios and one-, two-, and three-bedroom condos were made for lingering: ten-foot ceilings, oversized windows, and a partial gulf view from every private balcony. Each suite pairs a kitchen or kitchenette with a living area and sofa bed — room to spread out, cook in, and stay a while.

Step outside to a private stretch of beach, an outdoor pool with a sundeck, an on-site spa and wellness center, and round-the-clock concierge service. South Padre's best is just off the causeway.
