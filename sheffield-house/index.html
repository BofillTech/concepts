“The wrap-around front porch is the perfect spot to relax with a glass of wine with friends.”

The Sheffield House is a charming Queen Anne style Victorian home built in 1888 for Dr. J.E. Bennett. Enjoy the unique floral varieties found in our gardens, and the gracious hospitality that brings our guests back season after season.

Located just a short walk from Old Harbor — up High Street in the Historic District — within walking distance of the ferry, restaurants, shopping, and beaches.

Your Innkeepers

Ed & Diane Hayde
— and Nala

We have been vacationing on Block Island since the early 1980s, waiting each year for those few precious weeks in heaven. In June 2005 we were fortunate enough to purchase the Sheffield House.

Our love for innkeeping and this beautiful island is the driving force that inspires us to give every guest their own little slice of heaven. Ask to meet Nala — she loves making new friends and belly rubs.

We look forward to seeing you on the Block.
