Our Story

Nestled Amongst Rose Gardens &
Breathtaking Sunsets

Our unique family-owned and operated hotel has had the pleasure of hosting the island's visitors since 1987. Nestled amongst rose gardens and breathtaking sunsets overlooking the Chincoteague Bay, our property makes the perfect location for visitors looking to engage with the beauty and charm of the island.

The private marina is the perfect spot to view a rocket launch from Wallops Island or to cast a line in hope of snagging your favorite catch. We are a short walk or trolley ride to Historic Downtown Chincoteague.
