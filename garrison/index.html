Welcome

Sweeping views, manicured gardens, and the sound of the sea

The Garrison is situated on a grassy hill with sweeping views of the Rachel Carson Wildlife Refuge and Atlantic Ocean beyond. Enjoy 10 acres of manicured lawns and colorful gardens, a sparkling 44-foot pool, and a central location on Maine's southern coast — minutes from Wells Beach, Ogunquit, and Kennebunkport.
