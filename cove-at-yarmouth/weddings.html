Say I Do at the Cove

Weddings on Cape Cod

A Cape Cod Wedding, Without the Wedding-Industrial Complex

The Cove is a great fit for couples planning a relaxed waterside wedding — somewhere between a backyard celebration and a destination resort. Bring your party of forty to a hundred-and-twenty for a weekend, take over part of the property, and let the Cape do the rest.

I

Ceremony

Outdoor ceremony space on the grounds with a Cape Cod backdrop, or a smaller intimate setting on the pool deck. Indoor option at the Loft for off-season or weather backup.

II

Reception

The Loft hosts the reception — full bar, kitchen, and a setup that comfortably handles seated dinner for up to 120 guests with room to dance.

III

Stay the Weekend

Block suites and townhouses for your guests so the whole weekend stays in one place. Welcome dinner Friday, ceremony and reception Saturday, brunch Sunday.

The Atmosphere

A Relaxed, Beautiful Day

From the ceremony arch to the long tables and the first dance — here's the feel we aim for. Soft, intimate, unhurried, with the Cape Cod summer doing half the work.

What to Expect

How Wedding Weekends Work Here

Most couples book the Cove for a Friday-through-Sunday weekend. We hold a room block for your guests, coordinate dining and bar service through the Loft, and help you stitch together vendors for ceremony, photography, and music. We're not a turnkey wedding factory — we're a Cape Cod resort that hosts good weddings, and we're hands-on with each one.

  • ✓ Outdoor ceremony space (pool deck or lawn)
  • ✓ Reception at the Loft Restaurant
  • ✓ Up to 120 seated guests
  • ✓ Room block for the wedding party & family
  • ✓ Full beverage service & in-house catering
  • ✓ Welcome dinner / rehearsal options
  • ✓ Brunch space the morning after
  • ✓ Vendor coordination support
