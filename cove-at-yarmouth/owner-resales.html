What You'll Own

A Cape Cod Home, Yours Each Year

A week at the Cove isn't a hotel room you'll never see again — it's a familiar place that gets better the longer you own it. Spacious layouts, two full bathrooms, full kitchenettes, and the kind of light that makes Cape Cod summers feel slow in the best way.

Ground-Floor Living, Private Patio

Spacious ground-floor unit with kitchenette, separate bedroom, two full bathrooms, and a small patio that's perfect for morning coffee or end-of-day wine. Sleeps four comfortably with a queen bed and queen sleeper sofa.
