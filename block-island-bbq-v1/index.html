The BIBBQ Story

One of the world's all-time great comfort foods.

BIBBQ brings authentic Texas-style barbecue to Block Island. Set on the grounds of the beautiful Sullivan House Inn, we strive to deliver a BBQ experience of the highest quality — every brisket hand-trimmed and smoked low and slow.

Book a fun-filled BBQ under the marquee tent at the Sullivan House, or have it delivered across the island. Either way, you'll be certain to be satisfied.

"Worth the ferry, every single time."
