Reservations & Policies

Check-in is at 4:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Our front desk is staffed 24 hours a day, so you can reach us any time — just press “0” from your room phone.

Book online any time, or call (800) 343-3869 and press 1 to reach an Inn reservationist. We recommend calling directly for the most accurate availability, rates and room descriptions.

A deposit confirms your reservation. One-night stays are prepaid in full; stays of two or more nights require a 50% deposit.

Deposits are fully refundable, less a $50 administrative fee, until 15 days before arrival.

For cancellations 14 days or fewer before arrival, the deposit is refunded as a York Harbor Inn gift certificate, less the $50 fee. Cancellations within 24 hours of arrival are responsible for the first night plus tax. An early-departure fee may apply if you leave ahead of schedule.

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover. Cards used to confirm a reservation are processed the day the reservation is taken, and an email confirmation is sent immediately.

Two- or three-night minimum stays may apply on certain weekends, holiday periods and special events.

Most rooms hold up to four guests, with a $20 per-person, per-night charge for the third and fourth guests. Some smaller rooms accommodate two guests only.

Yes — rollaway beds and Pack ’n Play cribs are available with advance notice for $20 per night. For safety and legal reasons, please bring your own Pack ’n Play sheets.

Dining

We have three dining venues: the ocean-view 1637 Main Dining Room, the Ship’s Cellar Pub, and the Tavern at Chapman Cottage — with live music on weekends.

Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends and in peak season. You can reserve a table online or by phone, and the front desk is glad to help arrange dining during your stay.

Pets & Accessibility

Dogs are welcome at our Harbor Crest and 388 buildings with no additional fee, and we supply blankets, bowls, treats and walking maps. Dogs are the only pets allowed; they must be declared at check-in, kept off furniture and bedding, leashed in shared areas, and never left unattended in rooms.

Service dogs are welcome in all of our buildings. Emotional and psychological support dogs may lodge at our Harbor Crest building.

Yes. ADA-compliant rooms are available — please call the front desk at (800) 343-3869 for details, or select an accessible room online and read the description in full.

Location & Getting Here

We’re at 480 York Street, York Harbor, Maine 03911, on Coastal Route 1A — just 2.4 miles off I-95, directly across from a park and the ocean.

About one hour north of Boston, one hour south of Portland, roughly 2.5 hours from Hartford and about 5 hours from New York City. Full driving directions are on our Getting Here page.

Parking is available at the Inn for registered guests. When you arrive, our front desk will direct you to parking for your building.
