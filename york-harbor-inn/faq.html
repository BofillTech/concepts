Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions guests ask most about staying, dining, and getting to the York Harbor Inn. Still need a hand? Call our front desk — it’s staffed 24 hours a day.

Reservations & Policies

Check-in is at 4:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Our front desk is staffed 24 hours a day, so you can arrive or get assistance at any hour.

One-night stays are prepaid in full. Stays of two or more nights require a 50% deposit to confirm the reservation.

Deposits are fully refundable, less a $50 administrative fee, until 15 days before arrival. For cancellations 14 days or fewer before arrival, the deposit is refunded as a York Harbor Inn gift certificate, less the $50 fee. Cancellations within 24 hours of arrival are charged the first night plus tax, with any remaining deposit returned as a gift certificate. An early-departure fee applies if you leave before your scheduled date.

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. The card used to confirm a reservation is processed the day the reservation is made.

A two- or three-night minimum may apply on certain weekends, holiday periods, and special events. Saturday one-night stays are not always available — please call to check.

Rooms hold up to four guests, though some smaller rooms accommodate only two. There is a $20 per-person, per-night charge for the third and fourth guests.

Rollaway beds and Pack ‘n Play cribs are available with advance notice for $20 per night. For safety reasons, please bring your own Pack ‘n Play sheets.

Dining

Yes. A full Country Inn buffet breakfast is included with every stay and served in our ocean-view 1637 Main Dining Room.

There are three: the 1637 Main Dining Room with ocean views, the nautical Ship’s Cellar Pub, and the seasonal Tavern at Chapman Cottage.

Yes — live music plays on Friday and Saturday evenings, upstairs in the 1637 Main Dining Room and down below in the Ship’s Cellar Pub.

Yes. Takeout is available from the Ship’s Cellar Pub.

Amenities & Access

Yes, complimentary wireless internet is available throughout the Inn.

Self-parking is available on-site for registered guests at no charge.

Yes. Our Jacuzzi Center & Patio features a heated in-ground hot tub, a fire pit, and patio seating. Bring your swimsuit.

Yes, two ADA-compliant guest rooms are available. Please call the front desk at (800) 343-3869 to reserve one or ask about specific accessibility features.

Beach towels are available at the front desk (a $15 charge applies for towels not returned). A small number of beach chairs are offered first-come, first-served, so we recommend bringing your own.

Pets

Dogs are welcome in our Harbor Crest and 388 buildings with no additional pet fee. We provide blankets, bowls, treats, trash bags, and walking maps.

Service dogs are welcome in all of our buildings. Emotional-support and psychological-support dogs are welcome at the Harbor Crest building.

Location

We are at 480 York Street, York, ME 03911, on Coastal Route 1A — just 2.4 miles off I-95.

The Inn is about one hour north of Boston, one hour south of Portland, 2.5 hours north of Hartford, and five hours north of New York City.
