Inn Amenities & Services

A century-old tradition of comfort by the Maine coast — from a full ocean-view breakfast each morning to a new heated patio under the stars. Here is everything that comes with a stay at York Harbor Inn.

  1. Home
  2. Amenities

Included with Every Stay

Country Inn Buffet Breakfast

Every reservation includes our expansive full breakfast, served in the ocean-view 1637 restaurant.

Complimentary Wi-Fi

Free wireless internet access throughout the Inn, in guest rooms and common areas alike.

24-Hour Front Desk

Our front desk is staffed around the clock — press “0” from your room phone any time you need us.

Private Attached Bathrooms

Every guest room offers its own private, attached bathroom for full comfort and privacy.

Beach Towels

Beach towels are available at the front desk for your day by the water — just return them to the lobby hamper.

Concierge & Local Guidance

Our reservationists help you choose the perfect room and plan beaches, dining and York-area attractions.

Ocean-view dining, included

Wake Up to the Water

Start each morning with our full Country Inn buffet in the 1637 dining room, where the menu has been featured in Food & Wine, Gourmet and Taste. Then linger over the harbor before heading out to explore.

Explore 1637 Dining

On the Property

New Jacuzzi Center & Patio

A new heated in-ground hot tub, large patio and fire-pit area, steps from the Main Inn. Bring your suit.

Three On-Site Restaurants

1637 Main Dining Room, the Ship’s Cellar Pub and the Tavern at Chapman Cottage — all just steps from your room.

Live Music on Weekends

Enjoy live entertainment across our dining venues on weekends throughout the season.

Seven Historic Buildings

A collection of seven New England inns across two campuses along Coastal Route 1A.

Walk to Harbor Beach

Directly across from the ocean and a park, with the Cliff Walk and York Harbor moments from your door.

Beach Chairs

A small supply of beach chairs is available on a first-come basis — we suggest packing your own as well.

Comfort & Convenience

Pet-Friendly Lodging

Dogs are welcome at our Harbor Crest and 388 buildings with no extra fee — bowls, blankets, treats and walking maps provided.

ADA-Accessible Rooms

Accessible, ADA-compliant guest rooms are available — call our front desk for full details before booking.

Non-Smoking Property

The entire York Harbor Inn is a non-smoking facility for the comfort of all our guests.

Rollaway Beds & Cribs

Rollaway beds and Pack ’n Play cribs are available with advance notice ($20 per night).

Gift Certificates & Add-Ons

Give a coastal escape with YHI gift certificates, or enhance any stay with romance and celebration extras.

On-Site Parking

Parking is available at the Inn for registered guests — our front desk will direct you on arrival.

Celebrations & Gatherings

Oceanfront Weddings

Customizable weddings and intimate elopements all year long, with oceanside ceremony settings.

Social Events & Parties

From milestone celebrations to holiday parties, our team crafts memorable gatherings of every size.

Meetings & Group Retreats

An oceanfront setting for corporate retreats and group bookings, one hour north of Boston.
