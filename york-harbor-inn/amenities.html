York Harbor Inn

Heaven By The Sea

Amenities at York Harbor Inn

Everything that comes with a stay at our historic Maine seacoast inn — from a full breakfast and ocean-view dining to a heated hot tub, free Wi-Fi, and dog-friendly rooms.

Dining & Breakfast

  • Full Country Inn buffet breakfast included with every stay, served in the ocean-view 1637 Main Dining Room
  • Three on-site restaurants: the 1637 Main Dining Room, the Ship’s Cellar Pub, and the seasonal Tavern at Chapman Cottage
  • Live music on Friday and Saturday evenings
  • Takeout available from the Ship’s Cellar Pub

In-Room Comforts

  • Private attached bathroom in every guest room
  • Flat-screen TV, telephone, clock radio, and iron with board
  • Free wireless internet throughout the Inn
  • Select luxury rooms add jacuzzi spa tubs, fireplaces, ocean views, balconies, heated tile floors, and mini-fridges

Wellness & Outdoors

  • Jacuzzi Center & Patio with a heated in-ground hot tub, fire pit, and patio seating
  • Beach towels available at the front desk
  • A limited number of beach chairs, first-come, first-served
  • Access to Harbor Beach and the oceanfront Hartley Mason Reservation across the street

Services & Connectivity

  • Front desk staffed 24 hours a day
  • Free Wi-Fi in every building
  • Complimentary local phone calls
  • Guest computer for web searches and boarding-pass printing

Parking & Accessibility

  • Self-park on-site for registered guests
  • Two ADA-compliant guest rooms — call the front desk to reserve
  • Accessible routes from the public entrance to registration, guest rooms, dining, and meeting spaces
  • Seven lodging buildings across two campuses on Coastal Route 1A

Pet-Friendly Lodging

  • Dog-friendly rooms at the Harbor Crest and 388 buildings, with no additional pet fee
  • Blankets, bowls, treats, trash bags, and walking maps provided for your dog
  • Service dogs welcome in all buildings
  • Emotional-support dogs welcome at Harbor Crest
