Your Vermont Basecamp

Family owned for over 35 years — in the heart of it all

The Olympia Lodge sits off U.S. Route 7A in Manchester Center, minutes from Bromley and Stratton Mountain and just one minute from Riley Rink at Hunter Park. Spend your days on the slopes, the trails, the galleries and the shops, then come home to a roaring fire and a glass of wine in the tavern.

Minutes from Bromley & Stratton
