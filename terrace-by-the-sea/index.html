Terrace By The Sea combines deluxe motel comfort with the warmth of a Colonial inn — set peacefully between Beach Street and Wharf Lane, directly across from the Ogunquit River beach.

Mornings Begin
on the Ocean Porch

Each morning, step out to our sweeping wrap-around porch — where a complimentary expanded continental breakfast is served with a panoramic view of the Ogunquit River and the Atlantic beyond. A quiet ritual that sets the tone for the perfect Maine day.

Over 100 Years on the Maine Coast

A Century of Coastal Hospitality

Since 1920, Terrace By The Sea has welcomed generations of guests to the shores of Ogunquit. What started as a simple Colonial inn has grown into one of the Maine coast's most beloved waterfront destinations — and we've preserved every bit of that original warmth.
