A Special Place in Ogunquit

A Place Apart from
the Everyday

Terrace By The Sea combines deluxe motel comfort with the warmth of a Colonial inn — set peacefully between Beach Street and Wharf Lane, directly across from the Ogunquit River beach.

Our ocean view lawn is one of Ogunquit's finest vantage points. Beautifully landscaped grounds invite you to slow down, breathe in the salt air, and linger a little longer.
