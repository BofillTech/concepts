Ceremonies & Events

Say “I Do” Overlooking the Sea

Your Ogunquit wedding is made even more romantic with the Atlantic Ocean as your backdrop. Our beautifully landscaped oceanfront grounds are a beloved setting for intimate ceremonies of 20 to 80 guests.

We are pleased to provide lodging for you and your guests across our five distinctive buildings. We'll gladly recommend the finest local reception venues — and a local trolley makes for a charming guest experience.

Wedding Features

Intimate Ceremonies

20–80 guests on oceanfront lawns framed by the Ogunquit River and the Atlantic beyond. No ceremony venue fee for lodging guests.

Full Guest Lodging

Five buildings, many room types — we can accommodate your entire wedding party in one stunning coastal setting.

Book Early

Peak season weekends fill fast — we recommend booking 12–18 months in advance for summer ceremonies.
