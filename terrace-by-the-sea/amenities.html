Included Every Morning

Breakfast on the Ocean Porch

Each morning begins on our sweeping wrap-around porch, where a complimentary expanded continental breakfast is served with a panoramic view of the Ogunquit River and the Atlantic beyond.

  • Freshly brewed coffee & assorted teas
  • Assorted pastries, breads & muffins
  • Fresh seasonal fruit
  • Cereals, yogurt & granola
  • Juices & hot beverages
  • Served daily 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM
Our Grounds

Waterfront Lawn & Gardens

One of Ogunquit’s finest vantage points — our ocean view lawn overlooks the Ogunquit River and the Atlantic with a white picket fence terrace, Adirondack chairs, and beautifully maintained gardens.

  • Panoramic oceanfront lawn
  • Adirondack chairs & teak lounge seating
  • Private patio terrace
  • Beautifully landscaped flower gardens
  • Direct access to Beach Street
  • Private walkway to Ogunquit Beach
Seasonal Pool

A Private Retreat

Tucked within our landscaped grounds, our seasonal outdoor pool offers a quiet escape between beach days — surrounded by lush greenery and fresh Maine air.

  • Heated seasonal outdoor pool
  • Pool hours 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  • Open Memorial Day through Labor Day
  • Towels provided
  • Lounge chairs available poolside
In-Room Amenities

Comfort & Convenience

Every room at Terrace By The Sea is appointed with the amenities that matter — thoughtfully designed for a comfortable coastal stay.

  • Air conditioning & ceiling fans
  • Flat-screen televisions
  • Private bathrooms
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi throughout
  • Mini-refrigerators (select rooms)
  • Private decks or balconies (most rooms)
  • Daily housekeeping
