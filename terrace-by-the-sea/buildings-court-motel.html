Beach Street Location

Court Motel

The Court Motel is our Beach Street building — the closest to the ocean and the most convenient for beach lovers. Step out your door and you're moments from the Ogunquit River beach entrance.

With easy access to Beach Street's shops, restaurants, and the trolley, this building puts the best of Ogunquit right at your doorstep — and the sand even closer.
