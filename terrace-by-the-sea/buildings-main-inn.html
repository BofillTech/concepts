The Main Inn

Our historic Colonial inn is the heart and soul of Terrace By The Sea. Perched directly above the Ogunquit River with sweeping Atlantic views, the Main Inn combines timeless New England architecture with warm, welcoming hospitality.

Wake up to breakfast on the wrap-around ocean-view porch, spend afternoons in Adirondack chairs on the lawn, and return each evening to the comfort of a beautifully appointed room — exactly as guests have done here since 1920.
