Deluxe Motel

Our Deluxe Motel building offers the perfect blend of modern amenities and classic coastal style. Spacious rooms with private decks or balconies look directly out over the Ogunquit River and the Atlantic Ocean beyond.

Thoughtfully renovated with fresh interiors, each room includes contemporary furnishings, flat-screen TV, refrigerator, and all the comforts you'd expect from a premium coastal stay.
