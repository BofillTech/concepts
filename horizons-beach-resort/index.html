Welcome to Horizons

Enjoyment and tranquility, right on the bay.

Tucked along the quiet northern shore of Cape Cod, Horizons offers newly remodeled efficiencies on the beach, ocean-view units at the Terrace Dunes, and multi-room poolside suites — every one with a private sun deck overlooking Cape Cod Bay and its spectacular water views.

With three quarters of North Truro held as conservation land and National Seashore, this is a stretch of coast whose beauty and stillness can't be found anywhere else on the Cape.

The Horizons difference

The only place on the East Coast to watch the sun set over the Atlantic.

As the day ends, the ocean breezes settle and the sky turns colour over the water toward Provincetown — a west-facing sunset on the Atlantic found nowhere else along the entire coast.
