Weddings & Celebrations

Celebrate with us.

Ocean Vista is a breathtaking setting for your wedding, anniversary, family reunion, or other celebration. Between the private beach, the dune boardwalks, and our on-property banquet space, we can host gatherings of every size — from an intimate ceremony at the water’s edge to a full reunion taking over the resort.

No. 01

Beachfront Ceremonies

Exchange vows steps from the Atlantic on our private beach. We can arrange chairs, an arch, and a path through the dunes — or you can come and bring your own vision.

  • Private dune-side ceremony space
  • Chair & arch setup available
  • Vendor-friendly — bring your own planner
  • Sunset timing recommended
No. 02

On-Property Banquet Space

An indoor banquet and conference space for receptions, rehearsal dinners, anniversary parties, and family reunions — with the option to spill out onto the terrace.

  • Seated receptions & cocktail layouts
  • Conference & meeting setup
  • Terrace access
  • Rehearsal dinner-ready
No. 03

Room Blocks for Your Guests

With forty-six suites on property, we can hold room blocks of every size — from a handful of suites for an intimate gathering to taking over the resort for a weekend.

  • Group rates available
  • Studio through 2-bedroom suites
  • Walking-distance lodging for all guests
  • Custom welcome touches on request
