Welcome

A quieter side of the Hamptons.

Ocean Vista is an oceanfront co-op hotel set in the double dunes of Amagansett — one of the best-kept secrets on the South Fork. Forty-six individually-owned suites, each fully outfitted with a kitchenette, private bath, sun deck or patio, and views that reach to the horizon.

Cross the dune path to a private beach with chairs and umbrellas. Walk the Walking Dunes. Windsurf or kayak in Napeague Harbor across the way. Then come back to a heated indoor pool, a sauna, and the kind of unhurried Hamptons quiet that's getting harder to find.

The Suites

Individually owned. Personally appointed.

Each unit at Ocean Vista is owned and decorated by an individual resident — so no two suites are alike. What they share: a fully equipped kitchenette, private bath, cable TV, Wi-Fi, and a sun deck or patio facing the Atlantic.
