Six Layouts. Forty-Six Suites.

No two suites are alike.

Ocean Vista is an oceanfront co-op hotel, which means every unit is owned and individually decorated by a resident. What every suite shares: a fully outfitted kitchenette, private bath, air conditioning, cable TV, Wi-Fi, and daily housekeeping. What differs is the personality — some upstairs with a sun deck, some downstairs with a little garden, no two finished the same way.
