Getting Here

Getting here is easy.

From Manhattan, Brooklyn, or anywhere on Long Island — here’s how to reach us, with or without a car.

Hampton Jitney

The most relaxing way to get out from the city. Multiple pick-up spots in Manhattan and the Jitney stops right in front of the hotel. Select the Napeague stop when making your reservation.

Book Jitney →

Long Island Rail Road

Take the LIRR (Montauk / South Fork line) to the Amagansett station. From there: Suffolk County Transit Bus (631-852-5200) or the South Fork Commuter Connection (631-702-1753).

LIRR Schedule →

Driving from NYC

Take the Long Island Expressway (495) eastbound to Exit 70 toward NY-27 / Montauk. Follow NY-27 east through Westhampton, Southampton, East Hampton, and Amagansett. We’re on the right, on Montauk Highway.

Open in Google Maps →

From Montauk or Amagansett Village

We’re five minutes from downtown Montauk and ten minutes from Amagansett village — right on Route 27 in the Napeague stretch between the two.
