Frequently Asked Questions

Before you go.

Quick answers to the questions guests ask most often — from getting here to dining nearby. If you don’t see what you’re looking for, give us a call at (631) 267-3448.

Driving directions are available on our contact page, with an interactive Google Map. We’re located at 2136 Montauk Highway in Amagansett — right on Route 27 between East Hampton and Montauk.
The Hampton Jitney stops just a few doors down the road. The LIRR stops at Amagansett and Montauk, and taxis are available at both stations. Car rental agencies will also deliver and pick up a car for you at the resort.
Two restaurants within about ½ mile serve lunch and dinner. Goldberg’s Bagels is just across the road for takeout breakfast and lunch — and every suite has a kitchenette if you’d like to cook in.
No pets are permitted at Ocean Vista at any time. We know this is a hard one for some travellers — we’re happy to recommend nearby pet-friendly options if needed.
No — courtesy lounge chairs and umbrellas are provided on our private beach. Just bring yourselves and a towel from your room.
Yes — there’s a barbecue area and picnic tables available for guest use. Just bring the burgers and the coals.
No. Some have a queen bed and a convertible sofa, some have two queen beds, and we have one- and two-bedroom units as well. Each suite has been decorated differently by its individual owner. Some are upstairs with a sun deck, some are downstairs with a little garden and outdoor eating area.
You can swim in our heated indoor pool, body-board in our waves at the private beach, and surf the breaks just out front. World-class point breaks at Ditch Plains — home of championship surfing contests — are just minutes away. Kayaks, bicycles, and windsurfers can be rented in town.
We have our own tennis court on property. Horseback riding and golf are available nearby, and there are nature and dune trails for hiking in every direction.
All summer long there are arts and crafts festivals and harbor festivals across the South Fork. Visit museums, galleries, antique shops, and the historic windmills. The Montauk Lighthouse — the oldest lighthouse in New York State, authorized by George Washington in 1792 — is a short drive east. East Hampton offers some of the most sophisticated New York City shops alongside an array of local boutiques.
The Hamptons Classic Horse Show, the Hamptons International Film Festival, the Montauk Music Festival, the Authors Night benefit, and a steady calendar of harbor and arts festivals run from late spring through fall.
We open for the 2026 season on Friday, May 15. Come stay with us for Montauk Music Festival Weekend and enjoy our heated indoor pool and sauna! For additional information or wedding room-block inquiries, call us directly at (631) 267-3448.
