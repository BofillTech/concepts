Resort Policies

The fine print.

All rates are quoted in U.S. Dollars per night and are subject to availability and applicable tax. The policies below apply to all reservations — please review them before booking. Questions? Call us at (631) 267-3448.

Reservations & Your Stay

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Check-in: 3:00 PM — on the day of arrival.
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM — on the day of departure.
  • Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may be requested at the front desk on the day.
  • Guests must be 21 years or older to reserve a suite.

Rates & Occupancy

  • All rates are based on single or double occupancy.
  • One- or two-bedroom rates are based on four-person occupancy.
  • A charge of $35.00 per day is added for each additional person.
  • Rates are subject to change without notice.
  • Regrettably, we cannot guarantee specific room numbers.
  • Midweek specials are available May through September.
  • Group rates are available upon request.

Holiday Weekend Minimums

  • Memorial Day Weekend — three (3) night minimum stay at weekend rate.
  • July 4th & Labor Day Weekends — four (4) night minimum stay at the Peak Season weekend rate.
  • Shorter stays may be available — please call.

Cancellations, Refunds & No-Shows

  • A 10% cancellation fee applies for all reservations cancelled in writing at least 30 days prior to the planned arrival date for Off Season, or 45 days for Mid, High, and Peak Season.
  • No refunds, credits, or changes will be issued after 30 days for Off and Mid Season, or 45 days for High and Peak Season prior to the planned arrival date.
  • No-shows forfeit the full reservation amount.
Note: Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing — email is the fastest. We’ll confirm receipt within one business day.

Deposits & Payment

  • A deposit representing the full amount of anticipated room charges is required for all reservations of less than one (1) week.
  • Full payment is due when making the reservation.
  • We accept cash, American Express, Visa, Mastercard, or Discover.
  • Personal checks are not accepted.

Conduct on Property

Occupancy Limits

  • Maximum occupancy varies by suite type — see Rooms for each layout’s capacity.
  • No unregistered overnight guests. Only registered guests may stay in the suite.
  • Only registered guests may use property amenities (pool, sauna, beach, tennis).
  • Children must be supervised by an adult at all times on property.

Quiet Hours

  • Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM daily.
  • Please be considerate of neighboring guests and resident owners — Ocean Vista is a co-op community.
  • Excessive noise may result in eviction without refund.

Pets & Service Animals

Ocean Vista does not permit pets. ADA-defined service animals are welcome and must comply with our Service Animal Policy. Emotional Support Animals (ESAs), therapy animals, comfort animals, and companion animals are not recognized as service animals under the ADA and are not permitted.

Parking

  • All guest vehicles must be registered at the front desk on arrival.
  • A maximum of two (2) vehicles per suite are permitted in resort parking.
  • No oversized vehicles, RVs, trailers, boats, or commercial vehicles are permitted on property.
  • Park only in designated guest parking areas.

Pool

  • Pool hours: 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.
  • Swim at your own risk. No lifeguard is on duty.
  • No glass containers in the pool area — ever.
  • Children under 14 must be accompanied and supervised by an adult.
  • No diving. No running on the pool deck.

Beach & Common Areas

  • No loud music in common areas or on the beach.
  • No reserving beach chairs — chairs are first-come, first-served.
  • No personal grills on property — please use the designated grilling area.
  • Bonfires must be contained in a metal container (available from the office).
  • Pack out what you bring in — please leave the beach as you found it.

Housekeeping & Departure

  • Daily housekeeping is included with every stay.
  • On departure, please remove all personal items and bag any trash.
  • Excessive cleaning fees may be assessed for suites left in unusually poor condition.

Liability & House Rules

Property Damage

Guests are responsible for any damage caused by themselves, members of their party, visitors, or service animals. Charges may be assessed for repairs, replacement, excessive cleaning, or loss of rental revenue.

Lost & Found

Ocean Vista is not responsible for lost, stolen, or unattended items. Items found may be held for a limited period of time.

Illegal Activity

Illegal activity, disorderly conduct, or behavior that disturbs other guests is prohibited and may result in immediate removal without refund.

Right to Refuse Service

Ocean Vista reserves the right to refuse service or remove any guest whose conduct is disruptive, unsafe, illegal, or violates property policies.
