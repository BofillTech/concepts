Welcome

A quiet stretch of the Upper Cape, open all year.

Set on just over six acres in Sandwich — the first town on Cape Cod — the Lodge keeps things easy and unhurried for families, couples, and travelers alike. Free Wi‑Fi, on‑site parking, a heated indoor pool, and a seasonal outdoor pool mean there is always somewhere to land, whatever the season.

Rooms & Suites

Room to settle in.

From cozy standard rooms to oversized two‑room suites — many with kitchenettes, some with a private balcony over the indoor pool or a jetted tub. Most rooms are pet‑friendly, so the whole family can come along.
