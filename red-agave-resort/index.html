Where the trail meets the table

An intimate basecamp beneath Bell Rock.

The Red Agave Adventure Resort offers an intimate setting of two-story chalets and studios with incredible views of Bell Rock and Courthouse Butte, two of Sedona's iconic red rock formations.

With hiking and biking trails accessible from our artfully landscaped courtyard and leading into the Coconino National Forest, days of adventure and nights of peaceful relaxation around the evening campfire await.
