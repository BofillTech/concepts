Our Story

A quiet escape, where so many return year after year.

White Sands Resort is your escape to elegant simplicity in the Hamptons. Located between Amagansett and Montauk, our oceanfront accommodations offer a quiet getaway, just steps from the pristine sands of the ocean beach.

It's a short trip east to Montauk's authentic flavor, or west to all Hamptons locations. Nineteen beautifully furnished rooms and efficiencies — every one with TempurPedic bedding, ocean breezes, and the kind of unhurried mornings that bring our guests back decade after decade.

— Come stay with us and see why.
