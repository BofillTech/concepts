The Corner of Main & Clark

The white building on the corner of Main and Clark is under new ownership — and we're thrilled to bring a little energy back to the Bridger Valley.

Sitting right on the original Lincoln Highway and surrounded by decades of history, The Soda Station pours delicious caffeinated concoctions alongside a building that's served this town since the 1920s.
