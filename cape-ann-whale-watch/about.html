Our Story

History

Founded by Capt. Fred Douglass in 1979, we are the original whale watch company in Gloucester. 47 seasons of pioneering trips, 10,000+ voyages logged.

The Vessel

115 feet long. 25 feet wide. 33 mph cruise. The largest and fastest whale watch boat north of Boston, with a heated cabin, full galley, and 400 feet of viewing rail.
