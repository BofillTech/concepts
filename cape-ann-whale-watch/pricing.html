What's Included

  • Full 3.5–4 hour voyage to Stellwagen Bank Marine Sanctuary
  • Naturalist-guided commentary throughout the trip
  • Hands-on educational program for children
  • Access to heated cabin, sundeck, and 400 feet of viewing rail
  • Sightings guaranteed — or sail again free for life
  • Free parking at the dock

What's NOT Included

  • Food and beverages from the galley (available for purchase at reasonable prices)
  • Gratuity for crew (always appreciated, never required)
  • Souvenirs from our gift shop

Group Rates

Groups of 20 or more qualify for special pricing. Call us at 1-800-877-5110 to discuss your group, school field trip, corporate outing, or family reunion.

Children Under 4

Children ages 0–3 sail for just $10 and must be included in your reservation per Coast Guard regulations. They count toward our passenger manifest even though the fare is nominal.
