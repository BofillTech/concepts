Who You'll Meet on Stellwagen Bank

Six species. Countless moments.

Stellwagen Bank is one of the World Wildlife Fund's Top 5 whale watching destinations on the planet. Here's who shows up.

Most Common

Humpback Whale

Megaptera novaeangliae

The showstopper. Returns every spring to Stellwagen to feed. Breaching, lobtailing, lunge-feeding — these are the acrobats. Each tail is unique, and our naturalists know them by name.

Length
48–52 ft
Best Behavior
Breaching
Regular

Fin Whale

Balaenoptera physalus

The second-largest animal that's ever lived. Sleek, fast, asymmetric — its right jaw is white, the left dark. Tall narrow blows visible from miles away.

Length
65–75 ft
Top Speed
25 kn
Common

Minke Whale

Balaenoptera acutorostrata

The smallest baleen whale on the bank — quick, curious, and often solo. Distinctive white bands on the flippers. Surfaces with a sharp pointed nose.

Length
23–30 ft
Behavior
Curious
Common

Atlantic White-Sided Dolphin

Lagenorhynchus acutus

Pods of 50 to several hundred — bow-riding, leaping in formation. Sometimes we have hundreds on a single trip. Pure joy on the water.

Pod Size
50–500
Behavior
Bow-rider
Rare · Spring

North Atlantic Right Whale

Eubalaena glacialis

One of the rarest large mammals on Earth — fewer than 360 remain. Stellwagen is critical habitat. We follow strict federal viewing rules to protect them.

Population
~360 left
Season
Spring
Occasional

Plus So Much More

Pilot · Sperm · Sei · Blue

Once or twice a season the blue whale (largest animal ever to exist) shows up. Sei, sperm, and pilot whales pass through. Plus harbor seals, sea turtles, and seabirds.

Range
15–98 ft
Best
Late Summer
Your Day on the Water

From dock to whales in one hour flat.

Three and a half to four hours of pure adventure. Here's how it goes.

  1. 1 ⚓

    Board the Boat

    Free parking right at the dock. Crew checks you in. Naturalist briefs the trip.

  2. 2 🚤

    Cruise Out

    Engines fire. Out past Eastern Point Light. Naturalist takes the mic.

  3. 3 👀

    Arrive Stellwagen

    One hour to the bank. Binoculars out. First blows spotted. The hunt begins.

  4. 4 🐋

    The Show

    Two hours with the whales. Breaches, flukes, bow-riding dolphins. Pure magic.

  5. 5 🏠

    Cruise Home

    Galley still open. Stories already getting bigger. Memories that stick for life.
