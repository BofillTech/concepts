Free Parking

Free parking is available steps from the boat, in our lot at Rose's Wharf. No meters, no fees. Look for the Hurricane II tied up at the dock — we're directly across the street from the Crow's Nest pub (yes, the one from The Perfect Storm).

Directions

From Boston: Take I-93 N to MA-128 N. Take Exit 10 (formerly Exit 14) for MA-127 toward Gloucester. Follow MA-127 N approximately 0.5 miles to Main Street. Turn left onto Main St. Cape Ann Whale Watch is at 415 Main St, on the right.
