Meet the crew.

The most experienced whale watch team in New England. They've named most of the returning humpbacks themselves.

  1. Home
  2. Meet the Crew

When you sail with Cape Ann Whale Watch, you sail with the most experienced crew in New England. 90+ combined years on the water. 10,000+ trips logged. They've named most of the returning humpbacks themselves.

Captain Jim Douglass

Captain · Owner · Son of the Founder

Jim has been watching whales since 1979 and became a Coast Guard licensed captain in 1981. He has personally led over 4,000 whale watch trips. Jim is a graduate of New England College and served as chairman of the Cape Ann Chamber of Commerce tourism division. He carries on the family legacy his father Capt. Fred Douglass started in 1979.

Captain Nick Danikas

Captain · Co-Owner

A commercial sword fisherman and herring seiner from 1970 to 1983, Nick became a Coast Guard licensed captain in 1983. He has owned and operated his own commercial whale watch companies since then and has personally led over 4,000 trips. Nick brings decades of working-fisherman knowledge of these waters to every voyage.

Captain John Karvelas

Captain

Capt. John has been working at CAWW since 1994 and became a licensed captain in 1999. He's a graduate of the Landing School of Boat Building and Design. Don't be afraid to ask him about his humpback whale tattoo.

Tina McMahon

Head Naturalist

An Adirondack native, Tina relocated to Gloucester and has been working with Cape Ann Whale Watch since the early 1990s. She is a PolarTREC teacher who participated in a research expedition to Greenland, and a member of the Stellwagen Bank National Marine Sanctuary Advisory Council. Tina is responsible for cetacean identification on every voyage — she knows the individual whales by their flukes.

Jamie McWilliams

Education Coordinator

Jamie's passion for whales and teaching people about them is contagious. She leads the educational programs on board — hands-on teaching tools, 3-D models of the ocean floor, whale baleen and teeth to touch, whale song recordings. Trip Advisor reviews mention Jamie by name more than any other staff member: "Jamie, the onboard naturalist, was extremely knowledgeable and passionate about her work."

The Rest of the Crew

The deckhands, galley crew, and ticket office team aren't household names — but they're the reason every trip runs smoothly. The friendliest crew in Gloucester. Trip Advisor reviewers say it constantly.

"The boat was comfortable, crew professional, courteous and well informed."
— Brimel, Boston MA · TripAdvisor
