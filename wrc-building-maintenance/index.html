What We Do

Integrated construction & maintenance services for every stage of a property's life.

From turnover cleans during active construction to ongoing janitorial programs that keep occupied buildings looking their best — our team adapts to the scope, schedule, and standard your project demands.

01

Janitorial Services

Recurring cleaning programs for commercial offices, residential properties, and institutional facilities — tailored to your schedule and standards.

02

Post-Construction Cleaning

Rough, interim, and detailed cleans that transform dusty build sites into ready-to-hand-over spaces, from individual units to entire floors.

03

Floor Maintenance

Stripping, waxing, polishing, and deep-cleaning across hardwood, tile, stone, concrete, and carpet — preserving finishes and extending floor life.

04

Final Construction Cleaning

Punch-list ready detail work that meets owner walkthroughs and handover standards — the last 10% that turns a project into a finished space.

05

Interior Window Cleaning

Streak-free, detail-oriented interior glass care for storefronts, lobbies, and occupied office environments — on schedule, without disruption.

06

Construction Labor Management

Skilled, supervised labor support — staging, site coordination, daily housekeeping — that keeps general contractor timelines on track.

07

Painting & Wallcovering

Interior painting and wallcovering installation with clean edges and clean sites — refreshes that hold up to daily wear and high-traffic use.
