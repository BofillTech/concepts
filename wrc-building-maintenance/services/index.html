Home · Services

Integrated construction & maintenance services.

A multi-disciplinary organization delivering maintenance, labor, and finishing trade solutions — helping clients achieve operational and project objectives across every stage of a property's life.

WRC serves as an innovative provider of integrated building services and construction support, helping clients achieve operational and project objectives through a multi-disciplinary organization.

01

Janitorial Services

Recurring cleaning programs for commercial offices, residential properties, and institutional facilities — tailored to your schedule and standards. From daily porter service to weekly, monthly, or custom frequencies, our crews show up dependably and leave every space in top condition.

  • Daily, weekly, or custom-frequency service plans
  • Trained, insured, and uniformed crews
  • Commercial office and residential building programs
  • Institutional facilities: schools, healthcare, offices
  • Supervisor walkthroughs and quality audits
02

Post-Construction Cleaning

Rough, interim, and detailed cleans that transform dusty build sites into ready-to-hand-over spaces. We coordinate directly with GCs, project managers, and owner reps to hit punch-list milestones and deliver units, floors, or whole buildings that are walkthrough-ready.

  • Rough clean during active construction
  • Interim cleans between trades
  • Detailed final clean for handover
  • Individual units or full-floor / full-building scopes
  • Healthcare-grade preparation when required
03

Floor Maintenance

Stripping, waxing, polishing, and deep-cleaning across hardwood, tile, stone, concrete, and carpet — preserving finishes and extending floor life. Our floor programs are built around manufacturer specifications and the realities of your traffic patterns.

  • Hardwood, laminate, and luxury vinyl care
  • Tile, stone, and concrete polishing and refinishing
  • Strip-and-wax programs for VCT
  • Carpet cleaning, extraction, and restoration
  • Scheduled maintenance programs for high-traffic areas
04

Final Construction Cleaning

Punch-list ready detail work that meets owner walkthroughs and handover standards — the last 10% that turns a project into a finished space. We focus on the surfaces reviewers actually touch: glass, hardware, trim, and finished millwork.

  • Glass, mirror, and finished-surface detail
  • Hardware, fixture, and trim detailing
  • Finished millwork and cabinetry care
  • Punch-list pre-walk and coordination
  • Handover-ready documentation on request
05

Interior Window Cleaning

Streak-free, detail-oriented interior glass care for storefronts, lobbies, and occupied office environments — on schedule, without disruption. Our crews know how to work around tenants, guests, and daily operations.

  • Storefront and lobby programs
  • Occupied office interior glass
  • Conference rooms and glass partitions
  • After-hours and weekend scheduling available
  • Coordination with exterior window cleaning vendors
06

Construction Labor Management

Skilled, supervised labor support — staging, site coordination, daily housekeeping, punch-list technicians — that keeps general contractor timelines on track. We scale crew size up and down to match project pace.

  • Skilled laborers, painters, carpenters, wallcovering installers
  • Punch-list technicians for close-out phases
  • Daily site housekeeping and material staging
  • Flexible crew size matched to project tempo
  • Supervised teams with direct GC coordination
07

Painting & Wallcovering

Interior painting and wallcovering installation with clean edges and clean sites — refreshes that hold up to daily wear and high-traffic use. Our crews handle everything from single-room repaints to multi-floor rollouts.

  • Commercial interior painting and touch-ups
  • Wallcovering installation (vinyl, fabric, specialty)
  • Color consultations and specification matching
  • Clean-site protocols for occupied spaces
  • Multi-floor and portfolio-wide rollouts
