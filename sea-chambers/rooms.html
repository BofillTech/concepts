The Oceanside Building

Our largest building — most rooms face the Atlantic with unobstructed ocean views

Room Locations

  • Rooms 1–18: Ground Level — no stairs
  • Rooms 19–32: Upper Level — one staircase up
  • Rooms 33A–39: Terrace Level — down a ramp
  • Rooms 40A–42: Sea Level — ramp + staircase

Building Features

  • Guest Lobby between Rooms 9 and 10
  • Breakfast location with 24-hour coffee
  • Ice machines on Ground and Terrace levels
  • Accessible guest rooms available
  • King, queen, double, and trundle beds

The Poolside Building

Home to the Front Desk — steps from the pool, hot tub, and fire pits

Room Locations

  • Rooms 70–71: Top Level — full staircase
  • Rooms 72–76: Ground Level — stairs required
  • Rooms 77–78: Lower Level — no steps

Building Features

  • Front Desk by ramp or half staircase
  • Adjacent to pool, hot tub, fire pits
  • Sea Chambers merchandise and gifts
  • Various bed configurations

The Inn Building

Charming standalone building with larger suites and apartment-style accommodations

Room Locations

  • Rooms 501–508: First Floor — two steps in
  • Rooms 522–528: Second Floor — one staircase
  • Room 530: Entire Third Floor — two staircases

Building Features

  • Inn Breakfast Room in Guest Utility area
  • 24-hour grind-on-demand coffee
  • Self-service laundry (April – October)
  • Ice machine in Guest Utility Room
  • Spacious suites with full kitchens

Accessibility / ADA

Accessible guest rooms available with roll-in shower, ADA bathroom, and strobe alarms. Registration desk, self-parking, pool, and fire pit areas are all accessible. No accessible route to the oceanfront deck. Contact Front Desk with any questions.
