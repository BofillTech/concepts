Our Three Buildings

The Oceanside Building

Our largest building with rooms directly facing the ocean. Ground level rooms have no stairs — perfect for easy access.

  • Rooms 1–18: Ground Level, no stairs
  • Rooms 19–32: Upper Level, one staircase up
  • Rooms 33A–39: Terrace Level, down a ramp
  • Rooms 40A–42: Sea Level, ramp + staircase

Most rooms in this building have ocean views

Features include:

  • Guest Lobby between Rooms 9 and 10
  • Breakfast location with 24-hour coffee
  • Ice machines on Ground and Terrace levels
  • King, queen, and double bed configurations
  • Select rooms with kitchenettes

The Poolside Building

Home to our Front Desk and conveniently located adjacent to the pool and hot tub area.

  • Rooms 70–71: Top Level, full staircase
  • Rooms 72–76: Ground Level (stairs required)
  • Rooms 77–78: Lower Level, no steps

Features include:

  • Front Desk accessible by ramp or half staircase
  • Steps from the pool, hot tub, and fire pits
  • Various bed configurations

The Inn Building

A charming standalone building with larger suites and apartment-style accommodations.

  • Rooms 501–508: First Floor, two steps into building
  • Rooms 522–528: Second Floor, one full staircase
  • Room 530: Entire Third Floor, two staircases

Features include:

  • Inn Breakfast Room in Guest Utility area
  • 24-hour grind-on-demand coffee
  • Laundry facilities (April – October)
  • Ice machine in Guest Utility Room
  • Spacious suites with full kitchens
