Perks, Policies & FAQ

We've found that these small offerings and considered policies can make a big difference in our guests' vacations

We are committed to giving every guest the best possible stay at Sea Chambers. All areas and amenities are for registered guests only. Thank you for your cooperation.

Check-In/Out Cancellation Parking Pets Smoking Quiet Hours WiFi Housekeeping Accessibility Dinner Specials Front Desk Laundry

Check-In & Check-Out

Check-In: 3:00pm or later. Please call us if you expect a late arrival.

Check-Out: 11:00am. Cars may remain on property until noon on the day of check-out.

Cancellation Policy

Before making your deposit, carefully review our cancellation and date change policies. Regardless of unforeseen personal circumstances, our cancellation policy will be enforced. If you are unable to keep your reservation, we encourage you to send family or friends in your place.

1 to 7 Night Reservations:

  • 14+ days before arrival: Refunded all but $30. Date changes incur $30 fee.
  • 1–13 days before arrival: Refunded all but first night. Date changes incur one-night fee.
  • Day of arrival / early check-out: Charged in full.

8+ Night Reservations:

  • 30+ days before arrival: Refunded all but $30. Date changes incur $30 fee.
  • 15–30 days before arrival: Refunded all but first two nights.
  • 14 days or less: Non-cancellable and non-refundable.

Parking

We have limited parking. One vehicle per room is included. Additional vehicles must park off-site. Oversized vehicles (trailers, RVs, large trucks) cannot be accommodated. Please contact us before arrival if you have a parking question.

Pets

Pets are not permitted at Sea Chambers. Service animals are welcome with advance notice.

Smoking, Vaping & Marijuana

Sea Chambers is a 100% smoke-free property. Smoking, vaping, and marijuana use of any kind are not permitted anywhere on our property — including rooms, decks, balconies, and all outdoor areas. A $250 cleaning fee will be applied for any violation.

Quiet Hours

Quiet hours are from 10:00pm to 8:00am. Please be respectful of fellow guests at all times.

WiFi / Internet

Sea Chambers provides free wireless internet access for all guests. The password is provided at check-in.

Housekeeping

Our Housekeeping staff will clean and sanitize your room before arrival. Daily housekeeping begins at 9:30am and is not available after mid-afternoon. Guests must leave the room while staff is working. For items needed later in the day, please alert the Front Desk.

Accessibility / ADA

Sea Chambers strives to provide a comfortable stay for every guest. Available accommodations include accessible guest rooms with roll-in shower, ADA bathroom, and strobe alarms. The registration desk, self-parking area, pool, and fire pit areas are all accessible. Please note there is no accessible route to the oceanfront deck. Contact the Front Desk with any accessibility questions.

Dinner Specials

Sea Chambers offers discounted dinners at two excellent local restaurants: Clay Hill Farm and Jonathan's.

Available March 26 – June 25 and October 1 – November 14, 2026. Three-course dinners at $59.50/person or $119/couple (plus 8% tax, tip and bar drinks not included). Guests pay at Sea Chambers, and our staff makes the reservation and provides a voucher.

Front Desk Hours

Our office opens at 8:00am each day. Closing time varies by season (between 6:00pm and 10:30pm) and is posted outside the office daily.

Laundry Facilities

Self-service laundry facilities are available in the Guest Utility Room in The Inn Building, from late April to end of October, 9:00am – 7:00pm daily.
