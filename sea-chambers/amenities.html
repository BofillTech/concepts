Amenities & Perks

Everything you need for a perfect stay — all included, no hidden fees

Pool, Hot Tub & Fire Pits

Our ocean view heated outdoor pool and hot tub are enjoyed by our guests from late May through early October, weather permitting. Our two ocean view fire pits are available evenings throughout the season.

Pool & Hot Tub

  • Open 9:00am – 9:00pm, May 15 through October 12, 2026
  • Pool is heated to 80°F / 27°C
  • Hot tub is heated to 102°F / 39°C
  • Pool towels provided upon arrival
  • No lifeguard on duty — children must be accompanied by an adult
  • Guests welcome to bring food and drink (no glass — plastic cups at Front Desk)

Fire Pits

  • Two ocean view fire pits lit at dusk, weather permitting
  • Seating is limited and may not be reserved for more than 10 minutes
  • Turned off before office closes

Complimentary Continental Breakfast

Sea Chambers offers a complimentary grab-and-go continental breakfast from 8:00am to 10:00am, in two convenient locations for our guests.

Our breakfast offerings include:

  • Bagels, English muffins, and fresh baked muffins
  • Cereals, oatmeal, and yogurt
  • Fresh fruit and orange juice
  • Milk, hot chocolate, and teas
  • Individually-sized butter, peanut butter, cream cheese, and jams
  • Small selection of gluten-free items
  • Multiple grind-on-demand coffee machines — fresh coffee 24 hours per day
  • Filtered water dispensers in each breakfast location

Most rooms include a four-cup drip coffee maker, toaster, dishes, and silverware. All rooms have a small refrigerator and microwave.

Guest Perks

🏖️ Beach Chairs

We have a limited number of beach chairs, boogie boards, and beach umbrellas available to borrow from our Front Desk on a first come, first served basis.

☕ Beverages

Bottled water and other drinks available for purchase at the Front Desk. Grind-on-demand coffee machines and filtered water dispensers available all day in our two breakfast locations.

📚 Books

We have a book trading shelf at our Front Desk. Help yourself when you need a book or leave any titles you don't want to carry home.

🚲 Bicycles

Bicycles may not be kept in the rooms nor on the decks. Please keep your bicycles on your vehicle when not in use.

⚓ Floating Dock

We have a seasonal floating dock for guests to access the tidal river. Use at your own risk, follows pool area hours.

🎲 Games

Selection of board games, cards, and puzzles available from the Front Desk. All rooms have DVD players with a lending library at the desk.

🔌 Electric Cars

We've installed outdoor outlets for overnight slow charging in select spots. Please alert us ahead of arrival. $10 per charge.

🎁 Gift Certificates

Available at the Front Desk or on our website. The perfect gift for someone who deserves a coastal getaway.
