Southern Rhode Island's Best Adventures

Misquamicut Beach

Miles of pristine sandy beach, surf, and family fun just minutes away.

Watch Hill

Charming seaside village with the historic Flying Horse Carousel and upscale shopping.

Block Island

Ferry from nearby Point Judith to the island's dramatic bluffs and beaches.

Downtown Westerly

Wine shops, restaurants, arts scene, and New England charm.

Burlingame State Park

Over 3,000 acres of hiking trails, freshwater swimming, and camping.

Fishing & Golf

Charter boats, pier fishing, and several courses within a short drive.
