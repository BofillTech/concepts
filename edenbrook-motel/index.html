Where mountain air meets the sea.

A family-owned Bar Harbor motel

A quiet hillside, kept in the family.

Edenbrook is family owned and operated — forty-seven rooms spread across four buildings on five quiet, green acres above the village.

Twenty of our rooms open onto a balcony; a select few look out over scenic Frenchman Bay. Every room is clean and comfortable, with a private bath, electric heat, air conditioning, cable TV, in-room coffee, and parking close to your door. Two rooms are accessible.

Our unique hillside offers mountain air on one side and a fresh sea breeze on the other — and active days on the island are rewarded with a deep, restful sleep at night.
