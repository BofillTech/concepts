The Boutique

A little shop with a lot of personality.

Tucked along Montauk Highway, Dazies is the kind of place locals send their friends to. The selection is small on purpose — thoughtfully curated, easy to wear, and chosen with that effortless coastal feel in mind.

Stop in for a swimsuit, leave with a whole summer wardrobe. The staff knows the inventory, knows the fit, and isn't afraid to tell you when something just isn't it.
