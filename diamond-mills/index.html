Welcome to Diamond Mills

Wake to the sound of falling water.

Our luxury resort sits overlooking the Esopus Creek dam, with waterfall views from every onsite room. A family-run property that prizes wellness, locally sourced cooking, and the quiet splendor of the valley — this is a place built to slow you down.

Plush overstuffed duvets, fine natural cotton linens, and a rustic European design ensure a restful stay, whether you've come for a romantic country weekend or a family gathering.

Rooms with the river at your feet

Each room is dressed in European-inspired furnishings and a natural palette — tiled baths with radiant-heat floors, oversized walk-in showers, and a furnished balcony where the falls do the talking. Choose your view below.

Queen & King Deluxe

Our signature rooms — one king or two queen beds, a walk-in shower over radiant-heat floors, and a furnished balcony overlooking the waterfall. Deluxe rooms can adjoin for families travelling together.

  • One king or two queen beds
  • Walk-in shower, radiant-heat bath floors
  • Furnished balcony over the falls
  • Sleeps up to 2–4 guests
Check Availability

Queen & King Supreme

A touch more room to roam. Supreme rooms add an oversized, furnished balcony for long mornings with coffee above the water — the King Supreme trades the shower for a deep soaking tub and is ADA-friendly.

  • One king or two queen beds
  • Oversized furnished balcony
  • Soaking tub (King Supreme)
  • Sleeps up to 2–4 guests
Check Availability

Esopus Suite

A single-level suite with a king bedroom set behind French doors and a separate living room with a queen pull-out sofa — room enough for the whole family, and an oversized balcony where the falls run all night.

  • King bedroom behind French doors
  • Living room with queen sofa bed
  • Oversized furnished balcony
  • Sleeps up to 4 guests
Check Availability

Tower Suite

Our crown. A bi-level suite with a king bedroom and fireplace upstairs, a living room with a queen sofa bed below, two bathrooms, and an oversized balcony perched directly above the Esopus Waterfall.

  • Bi-level suite, king bed & fireplace
  • Two bathrooms
  • Living room with queen sofa bed
  • Sleeps up to 4 guests

The Waterside Spa

Rest is the whole point.

Our brand-new spa and wellness center is open seven days a week. Every room night includes complimentary access to the plunge pools and relaxation lounges — treatments are a pleasure away, and every chair has a view of the water.
