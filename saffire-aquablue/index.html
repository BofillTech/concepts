— The Concept —

Where the Beach Meets Bangkok

Saffire is the signature restaurant at AquaBlue Hotel, bringing the bold, layered flavors of Thai cuisine to Narragansett's beach district. Local Point Judith seafood, Rhode Island farm produce, and craft cocktails built around lemongrass, kaffir lime, and Thai chili — all served against the sound of the Atlantic.

Open to hotel guests and the public alike. Walk down from your room or come spend the evening — either way, you'll find a kitchen that takes Thai street food seriously and a bar that doesn't take itself too seriously.

Cuisine Modern Thai
