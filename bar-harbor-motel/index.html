Welcome to the motel

Where retro charm meets the great outdoors.

Adjacent to the natural beauty of Acadia National Park, our newly renovated motel is your home base for the whole Mount Desert Island adventure.

Spend your days hiking, biking, or simply lounging by the pool, then come back to thoughtfully renovated rooms with a little mid-century soul. The Island Explorer shuttle stops right outside our door, and a leisurely 20-minute stroll along the multi-use sidewalk lands you in downtown Bar Harbor.

For the nature lovers, a scenic one-mile trail at the back of the property connects directly to Acadia’s iconic carriage roads. Your unforgettable Maine getaway awaits.
