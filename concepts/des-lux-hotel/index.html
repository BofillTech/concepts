Welcome to the Des Lux

A landmark, reimagined for the way you travel now

Opened in 1912 as the Des Moines Club and known more recently as The Suites of 800 Locust, the Des Lux is one of downtown's most storied buildings — historic but contemporary, with personalized, concierge-style service at its heart.

Centrally located near the city's convention centers, attractions, and the Court Avenue and East Village districts, it pairs old-world character with every modern comfort.

Rich With Heritage

Four gargoyles, watching over you

Replete with rich woods, elegant brick, and a famous foursome of gargoyles keeping watch from the facade, the Des Lux carries more than a century of stories in its walls — one of America's fastest-growing downtowns just beyond its doors.

Step inside one of Des Moines' most venerable properties, where the grandeur of 1912 meets the ease of a modern luxury stay.
