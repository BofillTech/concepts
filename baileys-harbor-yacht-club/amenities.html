Accommodations

Staterooms

Traditional hotel-style rooms with one or two beds, optional kitchenette, private bath, patio or balcony, and harbor or courtyard views. All include refrigerator, microwave, and coffee maker.

View Staterooms

Junior Suites

Private bedroom and bathroom with a separate living room, sleeper sofa, kitchenette, and private patio or balcony. Spectacular harbor and marina views.

View Junior Suites

One Bedroom Suites

Private bedroom, full bathroom, separate living area with sleeper sofa, gas fireplace, full kitchen, and private patio or balcony with harbor or courtyard views.

View One Bedroom Suites

Two Bedroom Suites

Two bedrooms (or one bedroom and a loft), one or two bathrooms, separate living area, gas fireplace, full kitchen, and patio, balcony, or screened porch.

View Two Bedroom Suites

All units are individually owned by families who take great pride in sharing their homes with you. Rooms are meticulously maintained to our highest standards, though each unit is individually decorated — our photos represent a sampling of the available decor.
