A Sanctuary

Steps from the Ridges Sanctuary and Toft Point, our lakeside resort is tucked away in peaceful seclusion — yet close enough to all that Baileys Harbor and Door County have to offer. Whether you're here for summer on the water or winter's quiet snowscapes, you'll find a retreat that restores.
