Welcome

Where Gettysburg history meets resort comfort.

Whether you're here for family fun, the National Battlefield, a wedding, or a group gathering, the Eisenhower Hotel & Conference Center welcomes you. Comprising the historic Eisenhower Hotel and the Aspire Inn & Suites, our 100% newly renovated, resort-style property sits minutes from the major battlefields, the Eisenhower estate and farm, the David Wills House, and the shops and restaurants of historic Gettysburg.

Stay, park, and relax — a public shuttle reaches many of the area's historic sites, so you can experience Gettysburg at an easy pace.

105 acres of safe, serene, landscaped grounds in south-central Pennsylvania.
