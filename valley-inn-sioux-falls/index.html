Welcome

A quiet place to land, when it matters most.

The Valley Inn was built for the people who come to Sioux Falls with something on their mind — a family member in care, an early appointment, a long week ahead. We keep things simple, clean, and dependable so you have one less thing to think about.

Sixty-three non-smoking rooms, a free deluxe continental breakfast every morning, and a complimentary Sanford security shuttle to and from the hospital. Park safely, rest well, and we'll handle the rest.
