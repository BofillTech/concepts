Getting Here

Wilton Manors Sits
In the Middle of Everything.

The Island City is exactly where you want to be in South Florida — close to the airport, close to the beach, close to downtown Fort Lauderdale, and close to most of the dining and nightlife you came here for.

We're three blocks east of Federal Highway, on a quiet residential street between NE 21st Avenue and Wilton Drive.

  • From FLL Airport 10 minutes
  • From Fort Lauderdale Beach 18 minutes
  • From Downtown Fort Lauderdale 12 minutes
  • From Las Olas Boulevard 13 minutes
  • From Miami International Airport 45 minutes
  • From Hard Rock Casino & Stadium 22 minutes

Free, Private
On-Site Parking.

Every suite includes a dedicated, gated parking spot directly on the property. No street parking, no permits, no surprise fees. Most of our guests park their car when they arrive and don't drive again until checkout.

For groups of 4+ booking a two-bedroom villa, additional spaces can usually be accommodated — please mention it at booking.
