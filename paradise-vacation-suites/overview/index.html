About the Property

Welcome to Paradise.

A tropical apartment-suite property in the Island City of Wilton Manors — built around a tropical pool, with fewer suites and more space than anywhere else on the Drive.

The Property

Apartment Suites Around One Tropical Pool.

Paradise Vacation Suites is a small, deliberate place. Boutique apartment suites — six one-bedrooms, three two-bedrooms — wrapped around a heated pool, lush gardens, and a stand of palms.

It's not a hotel. There's no front desk lobby, no breakfast buffet, no parade of strangers wandering past your door. It's also not a generic Airbnb. There's a real on-site team, and a real tropical pool you can use whenever you want.

What it is, exactly, is a small luxury rental property — the same operational standards you'd expect at a five-star resort, applied to nine fully equipped apartment suites.

The Apartment Itself

An Actual Place to Live, for a Week or a Month.

Each suite is a real apartment. A full kitchen with real cookware, sharp knives, a proper coffee setup. Laundry on-site that means you don't pack like you're going to war — in-suite for two-bedroom villas, a complex-wide facility for one-bedroom suites. A dedicated workspace with a desk and chair and reliable wifi for the guests who work remote.

Premium linens, the famously comfortable beds (we get emails asking where we got them — they're Sealy plush pillow-tops, since you asked), extra towels, and a private patio outside.

It's the kind of place where on day three you stop unpacking your toiletries each morning and start using the cabinets like you live there. Because for a week, you do.

What Sets Paradise Apart

Three Reasons
Our Guests Come Back.

Most of our reservations are returning guests. Here's what they tell us when we ask why.

No. 01

The Privacy of an Apartment

It's not a hotel room. There's no shared corridor, no neighbor through the wall, no daily housekeeping interruption. You unlock a private door, walk into your own space, and stay there as long as you like. Many of our guests stay a month.

No. 02

The Service of a Resort

It's also not a vacation rental. There's a real on-site team, real housekeeping standards, Fresh Towels, and someone to call when something needs fixing — usually within the hour.

No. 03

The Walk to the Drive

Ten minutes on foot to the heart of Wilton Drive. Park your car when you arrive and don't drive again until checkout. The whole reason most of our guests came in the first place — and the reason they keep coming back.
