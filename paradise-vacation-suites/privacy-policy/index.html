Privacy Policy.

What information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated June 2026. Paradise Vacation Suites respects your privacy and is committed to handling your information transparently.

Information We Collect

When you make a reservation or contact us, we collect the information you provide directly — your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and payment details. We also receive information about your booking from our reservation platform (Lodgify) and any third-party channels you may have booked through (Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com).

When you visit our website, we collect anonymous usage data through standard web analytics tools — pages viewed, approximate location, device type, referral source.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your reservation and communicate with you about your stay.
  • To send confirmation, check-in instructions, and post-stay follow-up.
  • To improve our property and services based on aggregate feedback.
  • If you opt in: to send occasional updates about special offers or seasonal events.

We do not sell your information to third parties. Ever.

Information Sharing

We share your information only with service providers necessary to operate the business — our booking platform, payment processor, and (if you've booked through them) the third-party platform you used. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and contractual obligations to us.

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our guests or staff.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies for basic functionality (remembering your search preferences) and standard analytics (Google Analytics). You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some site features may not work without them.

Your Choices

  • Access: You can request a copy of the information we have about you.
  • Correction: You can ask us to correct any information that's inaccurate.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your information, subject to legal record-keeping requirements (financial records, tax filings).
  • Marketing opt-out: If you've opted in to receive marketing emails, you can opt out at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email.

Data Retention

We keep reservation records for seven years for tax and accounting purposes. Marketing list data is kept until you opt out. Website analytics data is retained per Google Analytics defaults (26 months).

Security

Payment processing is handled by our PCI-compliant booking platform. We do not store full credit card numbers on our own systems. Reasonable physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards protect the information we do hold.

Children

Our property is restricted to guests twenty-one and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, email paradisevacationsuites@gmail.com or call 954-868-5671.
