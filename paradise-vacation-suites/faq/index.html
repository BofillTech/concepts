Twenty years of guests, twenty years of the same great questions. Here are the answers.

Booking & Check-In

When can I check in?
Check-in begins at 4 PM. If you'd like to arrive earlier, let us know — we can usually accommodate a bag drop or pool access if your suite isn't quite ready.
When do I check out?
Check-out is 11 AM. Late check-out can sometimes be arranged depending on the next reservation; please ask at booking or the day before.
Do you accept walk-ins?
We can occasionally accommodate walk-ins, but the property runs at 90%+ occupancy — booking ahead is strongly recommended, especially November through April and during Pride weekend.
Can I book directly with you?
Yes, and please do — direct bookings get the best rate and the most flexible cancellation policy. Book through the Book Now button at the top of any page, or call us at 954-868-5671.
What's your cancellation policy?
Standard direct bookings: full refund for cancellations made 7 days before check-in. Inside 7 days, the first night is non-refundable. We're also reasonable humans — call us if something unexpected comes up.

The Suites

What's the difference between the suites?
Suites 01 through 06 are one-bedroom apartments (~800 sq ft, sleep 2). Suites 2112, 2120, and 2124 are two-bedroom villas (1,150 sq ft, sleep 4-6). Each has its own personality — see the Suites page for the full breakdown.
Are the suites really fully equipped?
Yes — actually fully equipped. Real cookware (not just one frying pan), sharp knives, a real coffee setup, a French press, dishes for at least six, and a stocked pantry of staples. Salt, pepper, sugar, oil, basic spices are all there when you arrive.
Is there laundry access?
Two-bedroom villas (2112, 2120, 2124) include in-suite washer and dryer. One-bedroom suites share a complex-wide laundry facility — detergent provided, no quarters needed. Either way, you'll never run out of clean towels.
How's the wifi?
Business-class fiber, dedicated to the property. Fast enough for video calls in every suite. We've had guests successfully run their entire remote-work setup from us for months at a time.
Do you provide toiletries?
Yes — full-size shampoo, conditioner, body wash, lotion, and bar soap. Plus a hair dryer, an iron, and an ironing board in every suite.

The Property

Is the pool heated?
Yes, year-round. South Florida winter pool temperatures are typically in the high 60s without heat — we keep ours at 84°F.
Pool hours?
8 AM to 11 PM. Towels are stocked at the pool kiosk all day.
Is the property age-restricted?
Yes — eighteen and over, please. We keep things quiet by design, so the pool stays calm, the gardens stay peaceful, and our guests can fully unwind.
Are pets allowed?
Unfortunately not at this time. We know — sorry. We can recommend a few excellent pet-sitters in the neighborhood if you need one.
Is there housekeeping?
Stays of 7+ nights include one mid-stay refresh (towels, linens, light tidy). Daily housekeeping isn't included by default — most of our guests prefer their privacy — but it can be added on request.

The Neighborhood

How close is Wilton Drive?
Ten minutes on foot — 0.5 miles via NE 26th Street. The walk is flat, tree-lined, and well-lit at night. Most of our guests park their car when they arrive and don't move it again until checkout.
How far is the beach?
18 minutes by car (4.2 miles via Sunrise Boulevard) to Fort Lauderdale Beach. There's an Uber/Lyft pickup spot right outside our gate; it usually runs $12-15 each way.
How far is the airport?
Ten minutes from Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International (FLL) — 4 miles south. Rideshare typically $15-22. Forty-five minutes from Miami International (MIA) — about 30 miles south via I-95. We can recommend a car service if you'd like a pickup.
Is Wilton Manors LGBTQ-friendly?
Wilton Manors has the second-highest concentration of same-sex households of any city in the United States (per the 2020 US Census) — second-highest concentration of same-sex households of any city in the country, in fact. Pride Parade in June draws tens of thousands. Everyone is welcome here, full stop.

Special Stays

Do you do long stays?
Absolutely — many of our guests stay a month or more, especially November through March. Discounted weekly and monthly rates available; please ask at booking.
What about Pride weekend?
The annual Stonewall Pride Parade in June is our busiest weekend of the year. Stonewall Pride Wilton Manors draws 50,000+ attendees. We typically book up six months in advance for that weekend. Reserve early.
Group bookings or buyouts?
We've hosted small wedding parties, milestone-birthday groups, and family reunions of up to 18 people across every suite. Whole-property buyouts are available with advance notice; please call to discuss.
Accessibility?
Two of our suites are on the ground floor with no steps from parking to suite entry. Bathrooms include grab bars; one suite has a roll-in shower. Please call ahead so we can match the right suite to your needs.
