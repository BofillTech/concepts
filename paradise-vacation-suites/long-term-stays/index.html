Stays of a Week, a Month, or a Season

Long-Term Stays at Paradise.

Snowbirds, remote workers, family visitors, medical recovery — many of our guests don't come for a weekend. They come for a month, a quarter, or the whole winter. Here's how it works.

TL;DR

Paradise Vacation Suites is built for long stays. Every suite is a fully-equipped apartment with a real kitchen, dedicated workspace, and business-class fiber wifi. We offer discounted weekly and monthly rates, accept mail and packages, and accommodate everything from a working week to a full winter season. About 35% of our nights are 7+ day stays; the average winter stay runs 14 days.

Who Comes for Long Stays

Four main groups make up the majority of our long-stay bookings:

  1. Snowbirds (November–April). The core of our long-stay business. Most snowbird guests stay 2 to 12 weeks somewhere between mid-November and mid-April. The classic profile is a couple from the northern US or Canada who has discovered that a furnished apartment with a private pool, a 10-minute walk to dinner, and on-site help is dramatically better than buying a condo they only use four months a year.
  2. Remote workers (year-round). Guests who can work from anywhere increasingly choose two or three weeks at a time in Wilton Manors. The dedicated workspaces, fiber wifi, full kitchens, and 10-minute walk (0.5 miles via NE 26th Street to NE 5th Avenue) to coffee shops makes this practical.
  3. Family visitors (year-round). Grandparents in Florida for an extended visit, parents helping after a baby, or extended family in town for a wedding or graduation. Two-bedroom villas absorb a lot of this.
  4. Medical and recovery (varies). Patients of the nearby medical centers — including Memorial Healthcare and Holy Cross — sometimes need 2 to 6 weeks of post-procedure stay. The full kitchen, laundry, ground-floor accessibility options, and quiet quiet 18+ environment make Paradise a logical choice.

Snowbirds — The Practical Stuff

Booking windows

Snowbird inventory is competitive. The earliest serious bookings happen in March-April for the following winter; by July most of the prime suites for November-March are taken. We strongly recommend reserving by Labor Day for any winter stay longer than two weeks. Returning snowbirds get first right of refusal on their prior dates, which fills up significant inventory before the public booking opens.

Discounted rates

We offer significantly discounted weekly and monthly rates compared to the nightly rates published on the suite pages. Call us at 954-868-5671 for long-stay pricing — we'll quote against your specific dates and suite preferences.

What's included for long stays

  • Mid-stay housekeeping — stays of 7+ nights include one mid-stay refresh (towels, linens, light tidy). Additional housekeeping available on request.
  • Mail and package receiving — yes. We hold mail and packages and deliver to your suite or set them aside for pickup.
  • Storage between stays — for returning snowbirds, we can hold a small amount of personal property (bikes, golf clubs, kitchenware extras) between stays for a modest seasonal fee.
  • Concierge basics — restaurant reservations, contractor recommendations, prescription pickup arrangements, transportation booking.

Remote Workers — The Setup

About 20% of our nights are now booked by guests working remotely. The setup we provide:

  • Dedicated workspace — every suite has a real desk and a real chair, not a kitchen counter and a barstool.
  • Business-class fiber wifi — dedicated to the property, fast enough for video calls in every suite. Speeds typically test at 300+ Mbps down.
  • Backup power — the property runs on the same grid as the surrounding residential area, with minimal outages.
  • Quiet by design — eighteen and over, no breakfast buffet, no daily housekeeping interruption. You can take a 9 AM call without anyone vacuuming the corridor outside your door.
  • Stocked kitchen — full cookware, proper coffee setup, blender, dishwasher. Lunch breaks become real lunch breaks.
Pro Tip
Several of our regular remote-work guests have specifically requested Apartment 04 (the Palm Suite, on the quiet east side of the property) — they describe it as their favorite "office away from home." We honor returning guest requests when we can.

Family & Extended Visits

For family visits — grandparents in town for a wedding, parents helping after a new baby, adult children visiting from out of state — the two-bedroom villas (2112, 2120, 2124) make the most sense. Each is approximately 1,150 sq ft with two bedrooms, two full bathrooms, full kitchen, and large living space. Sleeps 4-6 comfortably depending on configuration.

For longer family stays of 2+ weeks, the math frequently favors a two-bedroom villa over a hotel room, especially once you factor in restaurant meals saved by having a real kitchen and the absence of daily resort fees.

Medical & Recovery Stays

Paradise sits within a 15-minute drive of:

  • Holy Cross Hospital (Fort Lauderdale) — 7-minute drive
  • Broward Health Medical Center (downtown Fort Lauderdale) — 12 minutes
  • Memorial Healthcare System (multiple locations) — 15-25 minutes
  • Cleveland Clinic Florida (Weston) — 25 minutes

For post-procedure recovery stays, we accommodate:

  • Ground-floor suites available on request
  • Grab bars in bathrooms (standard in all suites)
  • Roll-in shower available in one suite
  • Quiet environment — eighteen and over, no families with children
  • Prescription pickup arrangements through nearby pharmacies
  • Light meal delivery coordination if needed

Please call us directly at 954-868-5671 to discuss specific medical-stay needs. We can often match the right suite and arrange specific accommodations with advance notice.

Practical Logistics

Mail and packages

Yes, we accept mail and packages on your behalf for the duration of your stay. We can also coordinate forwarding services if needed.

Laundry

Two-bedroom villas (2112, 2120, 2124) have in-suite full-size washer/dryer. One-bedroom suites use the property's complex-wide laundry facility — detergent provided, no quarters needed. For stays of a week or more, this means you can pack roughly half what you would otherwise.

Cleaning frequency

Stays of 7+ nights include one mid-stay housekeeping visit. We can arrange additional cleaning visits on request at an additional fee — typical schedules for long stays are weekly or biweekly.

Bringing your own stuff

For returning snowbirds, we can hold a small amount of personal property (golf clubs, bikes, kitchen extras, beach gear) between stays for a modest seasonal storage fee. Many of our returning guests appreciate not having to pack the same things twice a year.

Pets

Unfortunately, we are not pet-friendly at this time. We can recommend several excellent local pet-sitters and a few pet-friendly alternatives if you need them.

What it Costs

Published nightly rates start at $99 for one-bedroom suites and $170 for two-bedroom villas. Long-stay discounted rates are significantly lower than the nightly rates would suggest — but they vary by suite, season, and specific dates.

To get an accurate long-stay quote, call us at 954-868-5671 or email welcome@paradisevacationsuites.com with your dates and preferences. We'll respond with a custom quote, usually within the same business day.
