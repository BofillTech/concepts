A family inn in the valley

The heart of the White Mountains.

The Lodge at Jackson Village sits on the banks of the Ellis River — the same clear stream that runs off Mount Washington — in a village Yankee Magazine named one of the ten most romantic in the Northeast.

We are a smoke- and pet-free, four-season inn just two miles north of Storyland, minutes from the 800,000-acre White Mountain National Forest. Mornings begin with a homemade continental breakfast; afternoons unfold by the heated pool, on the tennis court, or down by the water.

A different valley every season.

Spring thaw on the river, summer by the pool, the most famous foliage in New England, and a village wrapped in snow. There is no wrong time to arrive.

Winter

Sleigh rides, ice skating, snow sculpting and free tubing at the Great Glen Trails — with the Polar Express a short drive away.

Village Events

Jackson hosts a vibrant year-round calendar — the Wild Quack Duck Race, the Covered Bridge Dance, the Jingle Bell Chocolate Tour and the Return of the Pumpkin People. Ask us about packages.
