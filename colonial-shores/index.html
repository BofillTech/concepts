Nº 01 — A Welcome

Your home away
from home, on the quiet side
of the Hamptons.

Nestled along the serene shores of Tiana Bay, Colonial Shores offers the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and coastal charm. Whether you're here for a peaceful weekend getaway or a longer escape from the everyday, our relaxing atmosphere and beautiful views create a stay you won't soon forget.

Choose from a deluxe bayfront motel room, a modern one or two-bedroom suite, or a spacious bungalow with its own private deck — all just minutes from the ocean, Main Street, and everything the East End has to offer.

Stay for the comfort.

Nº 03 — The Setting

Mornings begin
poolside. Afternoons
unfold on the bay.

Tiana Bay is the calmer, quieter water of the Hamptons — a wide, protected inlet a short paddle from the Atlantic, framed by salt marsh and sailboats. From the dock, you can see why we don't think we need to say much more.
