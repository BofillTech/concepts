Welcome to the Inn

Slow mornings, ivy at the window, and the gentle hum of an old river town.

Stay with us and relax from the pressures of everyday life. From individually decorated rooms to the peace of the garden terrace, the Inn keeps a slower, quieter pace — so you return home refreshed. We are nestled in the heart of Cold Spring among the shops and restaurants, a five-minute walk from the train and the river, and twelve minutes from the trailheads.

The Village

Cold Spring is the kind of place you slow down for

This quaint Hudson River town is home to fine dining, antique shops, boutiques, art galleries, theater festivals, historic attractions, and the trailheads that draw walkers up into the Highlands. Reach it easily by train or car from New York City, New Jersey, or Connecticut — then leave the car where it is.
