Our Story

A neighborhood institution, on Westchester Avenue.

Pelham Bay Home Center opened on Westchester Avenue more than 20 years ago with a simple idea: a Bronx homeowner deserves the same brands, the same selection, and the same expert advice as anyone walking into a designer showroom on Park Avenue — without the markup or the attitude.

Two decades later, we're still here. Still family-owned. Still answering questions about leaky shut-off valves and unfamiliar kitchen layouts. Still delivering across all five boroughs. We've watched the neighborhood change. We haven't.
