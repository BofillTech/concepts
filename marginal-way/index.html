— A Welcome from the Sea

Ogunquit's finest location, overlooking the sea.

Set on a quiet bend of Wharf Lane, the Marginal Way House has welcomed guests to the Maine coast for generations.

Wraparound porches face the surf. Salt air drifts through plantation shutters. Beyond the lawn, sailboats slip across the cove and the Marginal Way unfolds toward Perkins Cove — one of the most photographed coastal walks in America.

Every room here is steps from Ogunquit Beach. Every morning begins with the sound of the Atlantic.

— A walk into legend

The path begins at our door.

The Marginal Way is a 1.25-mile cliffside walk that winds from Ogunquit Beach to the lobster shacks of Perkins Cove. It traces tide pools, weather-beaten benches, and views that have inspired painters for over a century.

Our property sits at the trailhead — close enough that you can step from your porch to the path before the morning fog has lifted from the rocks.
