A charming Colorado bed & breakfast

Come as guests, leave as friends.

A true slice of nostalgia from the early ski-town days, with all the modern comforts. The Nordic Inn is the first and only remaining hotel in Mt. Crested Butte from the year the ski area opened in 1963 — beautifully renovated to keep the charm of the original lodge while adding touches of luxury.

Home isn't a place, it's a feeling. If you're after a refreshing getaway with genuine people, an authentic vibe, and warm cookies at the end of the day, you've found your place.

The Great Room

A roaring fire, and a seat with a view.

Begin each morning in the Breakfast Room with a wholesome continental spread before your mountain adventure. After a day of exploring, we welcome you back to a roaring fire in the Great Room — relax, eat cookies, play games, or just take in the amazing views of Mt. Crested Butte.

It's the kind of evening that turns a stay into a memory: a glass of something good, a warm hearth, and people to share it with.

Meet the Mountain Express

Park once. We'll get you everywhere.

Complimentary transportation is just steps from the lodge. Free buses run every 20 minutes in season, traveling between Mt. Crested Butte and the historic town below — so you can leave the car and explore.

Colorado's last great ski town

Steps from the base, minutes from the village.

At 9,898 feet, the Nordic Inn sits just steps from the base of Crested Butte Mountain Resort and a short ride from the historic town below — a U.S. National Historic Landmark District, and one of the last family-owned mountain resorts where the slopes stay crowd-free.
