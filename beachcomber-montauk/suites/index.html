Plan Your Stay

From cozy to stately.

Choose from a variety of suites — individually decorated, smoke-free, and outfitted for everything from a quick weekend to a long Montauk summer.

Welcome Home

All suites offer a sofa bed and private bathroom. Pick from Upper- and Lower-Level Studios, One- and Two-Bedroom Suites — including some with fireplaces — and our unique Hill House, with One- and Two-Bedroom layouts and full kitchens. Accessible rooms with wider bathroom doors, lower counters, and roll-in showers are also available.

A two-night minimum currently applies to all reservations.
