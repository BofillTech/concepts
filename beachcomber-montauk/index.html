A quiet stretch of Old Montauk.

Magnificent ocean views, steps from a secluded beach, and a quiet stretch of Old Montauk Highway away from the traffic. You can walk or take a quick bike ride into town. Visit great parks, museums, art galleries, go sailing, fishing, and more.

Affordable rates and excellent accommodations make the Beachcomber the perfect Montauk vacation destination.
