Why Beachcomber

The Beachcomber exceeds expectations.

Whether you want to work out or veg out, The Beachcomber Resort at Montauk has all the amenities you need.

Mornings to Evenings

Both, or neither.

Relax in our beautiful, heated pool where chairs, umbrellas, and towels are there for you. Tone up in our workout room, sweat it out in the sauna, or shore up your tennis game.

Enjoy the ocean across the street in our magnificent exclusive beach area, or mellow out and stargaze on your terrace.

The Beachcomber Advantage

Well-maintained. Individually decorated.

Choose from a variety of well-maintained, individually decorated, and airy rooms. Featuring free Wi-Fi access, full-sized kitchens with plates, utensils, stoves, dishwashers, refrigerators, cable TV, tea and coffee makers, and blow dryers.

Why The Beachcomber

Minutes from town. Away from the traffic.

You can walk or easily bike into town. Our secluded ocean area is just across the street, Hither Hills State Park is 5 minutes down the road, and scenic Montauk Downs State Park Golf Course is only a 10-minute drive away.

And don't forget the iconic Montauk Lighthouse, just a few miles beyond the town. Plus, Montauk's local dining and shopping await.
