All The Amenities You Need

Six reasons to slow down.

Heated pool. Private beach. Tennis court. Workout room. Sauna. Private balconies. Every detail of your stay — already handled.

Amenity — 01

A pool you can actually use.

Our beautiful heated pool is the heart of the property — chairs, umbrellas, and towels already set out, waiting for you. Whether you want to swim laps before breakfast or read a book in the shade until dinner, this is where the day finds its rhythm.

The pool opens Memorial Day and closes Columbus Day, so the swim season is generous on both ends.

Open Memorial Day — Columbus Day
Amenity — 02

Our exclusive stretch of Atlantic.

Step across Old Montauk Highway and you're on it — our private, exclusive beach area, just for Beachcomber guests. Quiet sand, clean breaks, and the kind of long views that make you forget what day it is.

Beach chairs and umbrellas are available for rent, by the day or for the duration of your stay.

Steps across Old Montauk Highway
Amenity — 03

Shore up your game.

A quiet, well-kept tennis court on the property — just for Beachcomber guests. Bring your racquet, schedule a morning match, and play without the wait list.

Amenity — 04

Stay on routine, or start one.

Our on-site workout room means there's no excuse to skip the morning move. Treadmills, weights, and the basics — right downstairs, no membership required.

Amenity — 05

The best part of any Atlantic afternoon.

After a long swim, a longer beach walk, or a windy round on the courts — there's the sauna. Cedar-warm, quiet, and exactly what your body asked for.

Amenity — 06

Your own front porch on the Atlantic.

Most suites include a private balcony — for morning coffee, afternoon naps, and evening stargazing. Our setting on a quiet stretch of Old Montauk Highway means the only soundtrack is the surf.
