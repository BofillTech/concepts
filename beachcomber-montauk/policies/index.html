Policies & Safety

A quick guide to our policies and safety program.

Know before you go — everything you'll want to confirm before your arrival.

Know before you go.

You must show a valid photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all special requests cannot be guaranteed and are subject to availability upon check-in. Additional charges may apply.

  • Check-in time is 4:00 pm, and check-out time is 11:00 am.
  • We welcome children of all ages.
  • Pets are not allowed.
  • Extra bed by request is $35.00 per person, per night.
  • Major Credit Cards accepted.
  • Swimming pool opens Memorial Day and closes Columbus Day.
  • Studios, Hill House 1, and 1 Bedroom Suites are based on two guests but can accommodate up to four guests for an extra-person fee for guests 12 years old and over.
  • Hill House 2 and our 2 Bedroom Suite are based on four guests but can accommodate up to six guests for an extra-person fee for guests 12 years and over.
  • One parking pass per reservation will be given at check-in. We do not guarantee additional parking spaces.

Cancellation Policy.

Deposits & cancellations

A 50% deposit is required for reservations of one week or longer. Payment in full is required on reservations of less than one week. Payment is due at the time of booking. A rebooking fee equal to 10% of the total room charge for the reservation will be applied to cancellations received thirty (30) days or more prior to arrival. No refunds, credits, or changes issued within thirty (30) days of arrival.

Additional information.

ADA Accessibility

The Beachcomber Resort at Montauk website is ADA-compliant, ensuring it's accessible to people with disabilities. Accessible suites with wider bathroom doors, lower counters, and roll-in showers are also available — please request at booking.

COVID-19

COVID-19 policies, social interactions, and workplace protocols follow current health and safety regulations. As guidance evolves, we update our procedures, following New York State, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), and the World Health Organization (WHO).

Service Animals

Smoother check-in: notifying our staff that you have a service animal ahead of time helps them understand that your animal is not a pet on our property without permission.
