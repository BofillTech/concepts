Your Home by the Atlantic

Two-bedroom beach-view townhouses in the heart of Old Orchard Beach, Maine.

Welcome to the Sands

The comforts of home, right on the beach.

The Sands is a collection of two-story townhouse condominiums sitting directly across the street from the beach in the heart of Old Orchard Beach, Maine. Each unit has its own full kitchen, two bedrooms upstairs, and room for the whole family.

It's the ideal family getaway — the space and privacy of your own home, just steps from the sand, the famous OOB Pier, and the seasonal Amtrak stop right outside the door.
