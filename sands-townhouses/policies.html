Reservations & Payment

Bookings require a 50% reservation deposit, taken at the time of booking. The final balance is due 14 days prior to arrival.

A one-time, non-refundable departure cleaning fee of $50 per stay (plus any applicable taxes) is added to every reservation. This fee covers the professional cleaning and turnover required to prepare the property for your arrival and restore it for the next guests.

Room rates are based on 4 guests. Each additional guest is $25 per night, with a maximum occupancy of 6.

Cancellation Policy

  • A $150 cancellation fee applies once a reservation has been created, up until 30 days prior to the arrival date.
  • Within 30 days of arrival: a fee of 50% of the total reservation.
  • Within 14 days of arrival: a fee of 75% of the total reservation.
  • Within 7 days of arrival: a fee of 100% of the total reservation.

No-shows — any guest who does not arrive on the reservation date and does not provide proper cancellation notice — will be charged for the entirety of their stay. We do not refund for early departures under any circumstances.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 10:00 AM. The minimum check-in age is 25.

A $25 lost-key charge will be applied to the credit card on file if a room key is not returned at check-out.

Occupancy & Parking

Each two-bedroom townhouse sleeps up to 6 guests. Rates are based on 4 guests; each additional guest is $25 per night.

On-site parking is limited to one vehicle per unit.

Smoking

All units are strictly non-smoking. Smoking of any material inside a unit will result in a $250 cleaning fee.

Pets

We love pets as much as you do, but we are unable to allow pets at the Sands.

Damage & Care of the Unit

If a unit is damaged or left extremely dirty during a stay, guests will be charged the cost to replace any damaged items plus $50 per hour in labor to return the unit to its original condition. This includes (but is not limited to) missing or damaged remotes, bedding, towels, windows, TVs, window screens, appliances, fixtures, furniture, doors, lights and mirrors.

Third-Party Bookings

Reservations booked through third-party sites follow the deposit and cancellation requirements specified by that respective site. Please review your booking confirmation on the site for details on its deposit and cancellation policies.

Please read and agree to all policies above before reserving. Questions? Call 207-286-4143 or email sandsbeachcondosoob@gmail.com.
