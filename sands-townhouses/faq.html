Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your stay at the Sands. Can't find your answer? We're a phone call or email away.

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 10:00 AM.

Each two-bedroom townhouse comfortably sleeps up to 6 guests. Rates are based on 4 guests; each additional guest is $25 per night, up to a maximum of 6.

We love pets as much as you do, but unfortunately we are unable to allow pets at the Sands.

All units are strictly non-smoking. Smoking of any material inside a unit results in a $250 cleaning fee.

Yes. The minimum check-in age is 25.

Every unit has a full kitchen (oven, microwave, coffee maker, toaster and place settings for six), two upstairs bedrooms with a queen or full bed, televisions in the living room and master bedroom, air conditioning, DVD players and hair dryers. Bathrooms have a shower only (no bathtubs).

The townhouses are located directly across the street from the beach, and just a short walk from the Old Orchard Beach Pier, shops and attractions.

Yes — on-site parking is limited to one vehicle per unit.

Yes. The Sands is adjacent to the Amtrak Downeaster line, which stops seasonally in Old Orchard Beach.

You can book online any time through our reservations page, or contact us by phone at 207-286-4143 or by email at sandsbeachcondosoob@gmail.com.

Yes. A 50% reservation deposit is taken at the time of booking, and the final balance is due 14 days prior to arrival.

A one-time, non-refundable departure cleaning fee of $50 per stay (plus any applicable taxes) is added to every reservation. It covers the professional turnover required to prepare the property for your arrival.

A $150 cancellation fee applies up until 30 days before arrival. Within 30 days it is 50% of the reservation, within 14 days 75%, and within 7 days 100%. Full details are on our Policies page.
