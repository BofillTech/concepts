The Two-Bedroom Townhouse

A two-story home away from home, directly across the street from the beach.

One home, room for everyone

A two-story townhouse, steps from the sand.

Every unit at the Sands is a full two-story townhouse condominium. Downstairs you'll find an open living area and a complete kitchen; upstairs, two private bedrooms and a bathroom. It's the space and comfort of a home, with the beach directly across the street.

Each townhouse sleeps up to six, so there's room for the whole family — and everything you need to settle in and stay a while.
